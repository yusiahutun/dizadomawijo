18 Facts About The Grinch (Dr. Seuss' How The Grinch Stole Christmas!)

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, April 7, 2024
Source: Tbs.com

The Grinch is one of the most iconic and beloved cartoon characters of all time. Created by Dr. Seuss, the green and grouchy creature first made his appearance in the classic children’s book, “How the Grinch Stole Christmas!” Since then, he has captured the hearts of millions with his unique personality and memorable story. Whether you’ve only seen the animated TV special or you’re a die-hard fan of the live-action film adaptation, there are undoubtedly some fascinating facts about the Grinch that you might not be aware of. From the inspiration behind the character to fun trivia about the making of the story, this article will delve into 18 interesting facts about the Grinch that will enhance your appreciation for this timeless holiday character.
