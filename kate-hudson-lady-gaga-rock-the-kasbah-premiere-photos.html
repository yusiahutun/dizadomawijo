Kate Hudson Stuns at &quot;Rock the Kasbah&quot; Premiere While Lady Gaga &amp; Taylor Kinney Pack on PDA

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

From make outs to show-stopping dresses, Hollywood's hottest celebs showed style at the "Rock the Kasbah" premiere in New York City Monday evening.

Kate Hudson, who plays Bill Murray's love interest in the new film, turned heads in a gold curve-hugging Balmain sheath dress with an open back and slit. The 36-year-old actress looked drop dead gorgeous, as the gown highlighted her hourglass waistline and toned body. She accentuated her look with a simple and sleek hairstyle.
