Best navy blazers inspired by Princess Kate: nautical jackets from H&amp;M, Marks &amp; Spencer &amp; more

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, May 1, 2024

When it come to her royal wardrobe, Princess Kate has a few main staples in her closet - floral shirt dresses, comfy & Other Stories jeans, Breton striped tops and last, but certainly not least, the Duchess of Cambridge classic: a tailored black or navy blue blazer for the perfect year-round nautical look.

MORE: Princess Kate’s cute £295 bag just got a gorgeous makeover and we love it

Princess Kate’ has been branching out into brighter looks – from yellow blazers to powder blue  – but she always relies on her go-to navy blue (or black) blazer, which is perfect for transitional dressing year round. They can can be single or double-breasted, and often feature gold button details.

So take a close look at the Princess' style and check out these royal-inspired options – including picks from Kate's favourites like Marks & Spencer, Boden and Karen Millen - to add to your own wardrobe, starting from £30 ($35).

Princess Kate's go-to Smythe blazer

The Princess of Wales's favourite single-breasted look is the £525 ($725) Smythe 'Duchess' Blazer - in fact, she loves the clasically tailored look so much she has it in three colours!

Shop Princess Kate inspired navy blazers, from M&S to Reiss

  • H&M Fitted Single Blazer

    H&M's Kate-worthy navy blue fitted jacket is lined and features notch lapels, jetted front pockets and a single button fastening at the front. It comes in six colours from ivory to black and pink.


  • M&S Collection Relaxed Single Breasted Blazer

    Also available in black, this single-breasted M&S navy blazer is described as having a "roomy relaxed fit". The jacket has two front felt pockets and is made from a fabric with added stretch for comfort.

  • RI Ruched Sleeve Blazer

    River Island's blazer features ruched sleeves for a relaxed touch, and a single button closure. The look comes in six colours, including navy and black along with beige, light blue, red, and white.

  • Boden Brighton Twill Blazer

    This fitted blazer by Princess Kate favourite Boden is made from a wool blend and is fully lined.

  • Reiss Haisley Single Breasted Blazer

    • How to wear a navy blazer like Princess Kate

    If you are wondering how to style a navy blazer à la Kate, we have some royal styling tips. The Princess often teams her blue jackets with a pair of skinny navy blue jeans and some shoes with height – like her favourite wedges – for the final touches.

    The Princess of Wales has shown this wardrobe staple's versatility – and how blazers are year-round winners from season to season – by also styling her tailored jackets over dresses, pencil skirts and even culottes.

    So now you know how to wear a dark blazer like the royal and where to buy one. It's a look that's easy to repli-Kate and will stay in style for seasons, and even years, to come.
