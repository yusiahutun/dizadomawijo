Tayla Damir is 'overwhelmed' with support as she thanks fans following pregnancy announcement

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 16, 2024

Tayla Damir says she is 'overwhelmed' with the love and support she has received from followers after announcing her pregnancy.

Taking to Instagram on Saturday the 25-year-old Love Island Australia star posted a beautiful picture of sunny clouds seen from the air to thank her fans.

On Friday, Tayla and her husband, AFL star Nathan Broad, 29, shared the good news that they're expecting a baby girl.

Tayla Damir says she is overwhelmed with the love and support she has received from followers after announcing her pregnancy Pictured: Tayla on the red carpet in 2022

'Overwhelmed with all the love, support and congratulations,' Tayla captioned her story. 'Thank-you.'

The reality star signed the message with a love-heart-with-arrow emoji. 

The AFL glamour couple, who have been married for four months, made the news official with a picture of Tayla posing with her footballer groom.

Tayla thanked her fans and followers with picture she posted on Instagram on Saturday

The pair can be seen touching noses as Tayla held a sign cut into the shape of a sun-flower with the message: 'It's a girl.'

Tayla also shared a photo of her ultrasound, as well as showing off her growing baby bump.

'Cooking for three!' she wrote in her caption alongside the image, in which the former reality star stood at a stove with her belly bared. 

The couple had a lavish wedding in mid-October, and since Tayla already knows the sex of her child, she is already several weeks pregnant. 

That means she likely fell pregnant on or shortly after her wedding day. 

The AFL glamour couple, who have been married four months, made the news they were expecting official with this picture posted to Tayla's social media on Friday

The couple tied the knot in the Gold Coast hinterland surrounded by family, friends and fellow influencers, all of whom were banned from using their phones as Tayla walked down the aisle.

She previously told The Herald Sun she and Richmond player Nathan were planning to follow the 'unplugged' wedding trend by asking guests not to use recording devices.

'When I walk down the aisle, I want to see everyone's face rather than the back of phones so in that case it probably will be a no-phone wedding,' she explained.

The couple tied the knot in the Gold Coast hinterland surrounded by family, friends and fellow influencers, all of whom were banned from using their phones as Tayla walked down the aisle

The bride was the epitome of elegance in a white floral gown by Oglia-Loro Couture, while the groom looked sharp in a classic tuxedo from MJ Bale.

The model's lace dress trailed across the ground behind her, and she styled her brunette hair in a neat bun which she covered with a long veil.

Tayla posted their beautiful wedding photos on Instagram on Tuesday, alongside the caption: 'Mr and Mrs Broad -17.10.22.'

Tayla also shared a photo of her ultrasound (pictured)

Other images showed the couple, who began dating in late 2019, posing atop a hill against the idyllic backdrop of the surrounding countryside.

Tayla also shared a blurry video of the couple with their arms wrapped around one another as they watched a fireworks display.

Tayla recently revealed she would like to get married before having children with Nathan, citing her 'traditional' upbringing.

However she made it clear she was ready to start a family as soon as possible.  

Tayla recently revealed she would like to get married before having children with Nathan

'I'm traditional in the sense than I would ideally like to be married first, but after that, let the baby-making begin,' she said in an Instagram Q&A.

The model announced her engagement to the three-time premiership player in June 2022, posting a video to Instagram of the moment her boyfriend proposed.

Footage from their home CCTV system showed Nathan surprising Tayla by getting down on one knee on their front porch. 

Nathan, who plays for the Richmond Tigers, and former Love Island star Tayla, went public with their relationship in December 2019.
