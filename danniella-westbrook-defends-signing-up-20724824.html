Danniella Westbrook defends signing up to raunchy site OnlyFans

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Daniella Westbrook has opened up about signing up to OnlyFans – insisting she won't be posting dirty snaps despite the platform's notorious reputation.

While OnlyFans is simply a subscription service, it is used by countless reality stars to flog X-rated images and videos of themselves.

Daniella, 45, has set her subscription rate at $13.99 (£11).

For that, followers have access to Danniella's page for one month.

Currently the account has four photos and nine posts.

Hitting back at fans who have mocked her for her latest business venture, Danniella tweeted: "So yes, I opened OnlyFans and the hate I’m getting from people is unreal.

"I’m NOT MAKING OR POSTING ANYTHING DIRTY SO WHY HATE ON ME OR ANY OTHER PERSON ON THE SITE.

"So if ya can’t play nicely don’t play at all."

She signed off the post: "Good night, god bless."

Replying to Danniella's post, one fan wrote: "Just ignore and block. They want a response.

"Don’t give them one. Life’s to short to waste it on negativity.

"Move on and don’t give time of day. Take care."

Another added: "The world is full of haters, usually people who are failures and so spend their lives judging people trying to actually do something with their lives."

Other famous faces who are currently on OnlyFans include Ex On The Beach hunk Sean Pratt, Love Island babe Katie Salmon and Big Brother legend Bex Shiner.

Former X Factor star Chloe Khan is also on the suggestive site.

The wannabe is asking fans to shell out £15.15 per month in return for graphic content.
