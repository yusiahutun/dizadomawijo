Women told to avoid dog sex to stop 'strange sicknesses'

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, February 1, 2024

Women have been warned not to sleep with their pet dogs as concerns grow over 'strange illnesses' that come from human-animal intercourse.

Police in Nigeria issued the bizarre after a viral video showed a woman boasting about a canine encounter, according to The Nation.

Provocative TikTok user Veegodess courted huge controversy online after claiming she was once given 1.7million Nigerian naira (£3,279) to have sex with a dog.

She said in the video: “What is the big deal there? I only slept with a dog, I didn’t kill somebody. You, in your life, you have done worse and besides, have you seen N1.7million before?
