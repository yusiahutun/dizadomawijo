Three Arkansas State's basketball players 'are arrested on theft charges'

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, March 28, 2024

Three members of Arkansas State's basketball team were arrested for theft Saturday night, according to a report. 

Dyondre Dominguez, 22, Julian Lual, 21, and Terrance Ford, 19, were booked on a charge of theft of $1,000 or less, ESPN reported. 

The report added that the trio were released around two hours later, according to  Craighead County (Arkansas) Sheriff's Office online records.

The theft was reportedly from a local store with the trio booked at the Craighead County Jail at 6:37pm. 

'The Athletics Department and Coach [Bryan] Hodgson are aware of the situation and are in the process of gathering all the information,' Arkansas State said in a statement.

'Once all the details have been obtained, the matter will be addressed in an appropriate manner.' 

The three players are awaiting a PC hearing on Monday, the Jonesboro Police Department said via ABC. 

Lual and Ford had finished their first seasons with the Red Wolves this year, playing 32 games each.

The 6-foot-1 Ford was the team's third-leading scorer through the 2022-23 seasons.  

Meanwhile Dominguez transferred to Arkansas State from UMass in April and is one of seven transfer portal players committed for next season.
