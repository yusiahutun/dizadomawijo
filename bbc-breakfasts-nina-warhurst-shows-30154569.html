BBC Breakfast's Nina Warhurst shows off blossoming bump after heartbreaking loss

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 30, 2024

BBC Breakfast’s Nina Warhurst showed off her blossoming baby bump as she nears the end of her third pregnancy.

The journalist took to her Instagram Stories on Sunday (June 4) after filming the beloved morning show with her colleague Ben Boules.

In the cheeky snap, the brunette beauty could be seen cradling her stomach as her co-host was seen going over papers on the signature red sofa.

She cheekily captioned the post: “Mum and Dad have lost the car keys again [eye-rolling emojis]” as she fondly looked over at the presenter.

Nina could be seen wearing a blue flowy dress which grazed her blossoming stomach which she teamed with a pair of green high heels.

The newsreader followed the snap up with a selfie of the presenting duo on set as she teased the newspaper headlines to her 43,500 followers online.

Earlier this spring, the TV presenter revealed the sex of her third child after enjoying a night out at a Self Esteem gig.

Reposting a video of her dancing to the track ‘I Do This All The Time’, Nina appeared to confirm that she is expecting a baby girl later this summer.

In the footage, she can be seen showing off her stomach while swaying to the live music at the concert, which she captioned: “One for the baby girl. First gig!”

It comes after she shared her heartbreak over losing three loved ones in the space of just a few months.

The 42-year-old broadcaster, who is currently pregnant with her third child, took to Instagram on Saturday to share her “three goodbyes” to her loved ones in a touching tribute.

Taking to Instagram, the newsreader – who has worked on the BBC morning show since 2008 – shared a snap of her posing alongside her mum and sisters at a memorial service.

She told her 21,000 followers that the service, which she titled a “memorial celebration”, took place on a glorious sunny day in the heart of Bath where attendees were banned from wearing black.

The doting mum wrote: “With my Mum sisters at a memorial celebration yesterday (‘no black please’) We’ve said three big goodbyes in the last few months to people we loved very much.

“And they loved us since we were kids – guided us, made us laugh, helped us feel safe. It’s been bruising having a run of grief, but made me appreciative of the support you get from the special elders of your extended village – whatever that is and whoever they are.”

In the family snap, Nina can be seen beaming beside her family wearing a colourful spotted dress as she showed off her blossoming baby bump.

The journalist insisted that the emotional day was one of celebration, and focused on making sure their goodbyes to loved ones were “full of gratitude”.

She went on: “We’ve also celebrated! It’s made me realise that lots of the tears are because the right farewells are full of gratitude and are something beautiful.

“A reflection of our clan; when the wake invite said ‘Carriages at 6pm' two of my aunties thought ‘Carriages’ was a hot venue in Bath for an after party. Yes Andrea and Jayne!”
