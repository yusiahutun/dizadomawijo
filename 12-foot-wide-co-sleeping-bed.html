12-Foot-Wide Co-Sleeping Bed - Large Mattress For Co-Sleeping

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, March 1, 2024

It may be called a "family size" mattress, but this massive 12-foot-wide bed from Ace Collection might just be the thing that saves your marriage if you have a spouse that tosses and turns all night.

This mega-mattress is a whopping 144 inches wide and 80 inches long, so you can get 12 feet of separation from your snoring spouse while maintaining plenty of room in between. To put that into perspective, it's almost twice as big as a traditional king bed.
