Explore Natalie Portman's incredible new 5million California mansion

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, May 8, 2024

See photos of the Black Swan star's new home

    Natalie Portman has invested in an incredible £5million ($6.5million) mansion located near the Californian coast in Montecito, close to Santa Barbara. The mother-of-two and her husband Benjamin Millepied have found the perfect retreat in the four bedroom, five bathroom property, which sits on a 10.4 acre estate overlooking the Pacific Ocean.

    MORE: See other celebrity homes here

    The property is built from steel, glass and concrete with rooms spread over one level on a 3,991 square foot estate. It has a modern and neutral décor with a state-of-the-art kitchen, spa-style bathrooms and a huge outdoor terrace complete with an al fresco dining area, swimming pool and beautiful landscaped gardens.

    According to property website Trulia, the mansion will serve as a holiday home for Natalie and her family, who live in the Los Feliz area of Los Angeles, where she is neighbours with Angelina Jolie. Click through the gallery to see inside the latest addition to Natalie's property portfolio…

    Photos: Trulia

    Natalie's new property has four bedrooms, including this small guestroom which features a double bed and direct access on to the home's 10.4 acres of gardens via a glass patio door. The room would be ideal for Natalie and Benjamin's guests, or could be converted for the actress' two children Aleph and Amalia.

    Photo: Trulia

    The master bedroom of the property also has huge sliding glass doors that lead onto the terrace, offering enviable views across the nearby Pacific Ocean. As well as having a large king-size bed and separate seating area, the bedroom also has an en-suite bathroom with a free-standing bath tub and wet room.

    Photo: Trulia

    The property has an open plan design with a modern kitchen complete with breakfast bar at the centre, which is surrounded by a relaxed seating area with armchairs and coffee table for Natalie and her family to relax at. The property has a modern design with a steel and glass roof, and retractable doors that mean the entire home can be opened up to lead onto the outdoor terrace and gardens.

    Photo: Trulia

    On the other side of the kitchen there is lots more space including a dining table and separate living room area with sofas, armchairs and coffee table. The entire property offers uninterrupted sea views and leads onto the garden and swimming pool – the perfect place for the Portman-Millepied family to relax and unwind in complete privacy.

    Photo: Trulia

    The property also has a separate living room complete with floor-to-ceiling book shelves and an open fireplace. This room could be ideal for Natalie to rehearse lines and research for her upcoming film projects, or the couple may decide to convert it into a playroom for their two children to enjoy.

    Photo: Trulia

    Outside in Natalie's new property's huge gardens there are several separate seating areas, all surrounded by lush greenery and palm trees for complete privacy. This outdoor terrace has a BBQ and dining table where the family can make the most of the Californian weather with some al fresco meals.

    Photo: Trulia

    This idyllic spot at the rear of the house is situated near to the small swimming pool, with breathtaking views out to the sea. Natalie's home is close to Santa Barbara and the Californian coastline, so she will be able to enjoy plenty of trips to the beach with her family should she desire.

    Photo: Trulia
