Can YOU spot what's wrong with this bedroom snap?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, June 28, 2024

A house-proud student sent a seemingly harmless picture of her newly-designed bedroom to her mother, only for her to spot a raunchy accessory attached to the bed. 

Ella Martine, a singer-songwriter, from California, shared the hilarious conversation with her mother on Twitter - and racked up well over 100,000 likes for her troubles.

While Ella was hoping for admiring comments on her new surroundings, her eagle-eyed mother spotted a pair of handcuffs dangling from the headboard. 

'Mom's been asking for pics of my new room... finally sent one... i’m so stupid,' she wrote alongside screen shots of the text exchange with her mother. 

Ella Martine, a singer-songwriter, from California, sent her mother an image of her new bedroom hoping for compliments on her decor - but failed to spot her own blunder in the snap

The screen shots showed the confused mother asking 'what's up with that?' upon noticing the hand cuffs. 

Ella said later in the thread that she had to get creative with her excuse, and told her mother that the cuffs were there because her friend used her room to shoot a short film in.
