The top 15 most expensive Nike shoes in the world, ranked

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, February 4, 2024

Nike is one of the most popular shoe brands in the world. It is not surprising that most of the world's most expensive shoes are from this brand. The fascination people have with these shoes and how they keep up with footwear trends is amazing. The most expensive Nike shoes available worldwide are proof of this.

Are you are a fan of footwear and would give anything to have the latest shoes? If this sounds like you, then you are probably going to love the collection of Nike's best shoes over the years. Some of these shoes continue to top the world's list of expensive footwear simply because of how unique and exceptional they are.

Most expensive Nikes ever

If you love Nike shoes and own a few pairs, then you would marvel at some of the world's expensive collection from the brand. Every shoe collector longs to have a pair of some of the most expensive shoes not only for their value but also for prestige. These are some of the most valuable Nike ever.

1. Roger Federer - $ 23 million

These are customized shoes specially made for the lawn tennis star. His association with Nike has always been phenomenal, hence it is not surprising that he owns one of the most expensive shoes from the brand. The shoe is not only stylish but is also designed to offer comfort to the wearer.

It also promises to give you colour, style, and outlook which are the top traits for the 2019 winner. It was the most expensive Nike shoe in 2019. If you are willing to spend 23 million dollars on a pair of shoes then this is the ideal shoe to select.

2. Kevin Durant - $21 million

These shoes make it to the top list of the most expensive Nike shoes. They were first customized for Kevin Durant, a basketball player. They're among the most expensive pair of running shoes existing in the world today. The shoes, just like the star they are named after, are bold and stylish. You can get them on the Nike website and they can be customized to your preference.

3. Kobe Bryant - $20 million

You probably know who the late Kobe is if you are a basketball fan. The news of his demise was devastating but it appears as though fans have a lot to remember him by, including getting a pair of of the most expensive pair of Nikes in his name. These shoes were created in honour of the star for his contributions to the sport and are the most expensive Nike trainers ever.

4. Maria Sharapova - $18 million

Women are not left behind in Nikes creations. You have probably heard about Maria, a popular tennis athlete. These shoes are representative of chic style yet functional and sturdy. You can order a customized pair from Nike's online store.

5. Derek Jeter - $16 million

If you love basketball and are a keen follower of the stars then you have probably heard about Derek, the popular world series champion. As usual, Nike took the lead and designed a special pair of shoes for him, worth 16 million US dollars. It's the most expensive Nike sneaker in the market.

6. Rafael Nadal - $15 million

Sportsmen are among the people who wear some of the really expensive Nike shoes around. This may be a marketing strategy but they still need to stand out and Nike does an excellent job in making sure this happens. Rafael, a popular tennis star, also has a Nike shoe to his name. It goes for 15 million US dollars and can be customized to match your taste and preference.

7. Lebron James - $12 million

It would have been strange if the Lakers basketball star would miss in this list, considering his popularity and excellence in the world of basketball. Clearly, Nike prefers to work with achieving stars whose success makes them stand out. His shoes may not be the priciest, but they remain one of the tastefully designed pair of shoes even today.

8. Tiger Woods - $10 million

The golfing legend also has a pair of Nikes to his name. These pair was specially designed to suit his golfing need. While the shoe was designed years ago, it has remained on the charts as one of the most expensive Nike shoes for the longest time. You can always get it in a colour and design that you prefer since it comes in a customizable category.

9. Rory Mcilroy - $6 million

Rory Mcilroy is a renowned golf champion whose success put him on the map. The star got the honours of having Nike make a special pair of shoe in his name. It featured as one of the most expensive shoes in the 2019 collection.

10. Michael Jordan - $4 million

Jordan needs no introduction in the basketball world. He is a legendary player whose efforts opened doors for many after him. He has been associated with the Nike brand for a year and has been doing a good job at it. If you want to own a Jordan pair then consider checking it out on Nike's website.

11. Air Jordan Silver - $60,000

This is one of the earliest Nike shoes considering it was launched in 1985. Surprisingly, even with newer creations, the Air Jordan Silver still remains one of the most expensive athletic shoes created by Nike. It is one of the rare shoes available today and was actually signed by the legendary Michael Jordan himself.

12. Nike So Cal Air Force 1 Supreme Max - $50,000

The Nike So Cal Air Force 1 Supreme Max also features among the rare Nike collections. Even so, these athletic sneakers that were released back in 1982 remain one of the most phenomenal pairs to own. Chances are that Nike shoe collectors would give anything to own this pair for their stash considering the fact that it was a one-off pair.

13. Nike Air Mag Back to the Future BTTF 2016 - $26,000

While the actual cost of this pair is $10 dollars, it still made its come back as one of the most expensive Nike pairs in 2016 at a cost of 26,000 dollars. This ancient pair may be considered a collector's favourite. The modern version is a replica of the original brand first created by Nike.

14. Nike Dunk Low SB Paris – 2002 - 11,495 pounds

This shoe makes it to the list because it is one of the limited editions ever made by Nike. Only two pairs of these sneakers were made. A pair goes for 11,495 pounds. It is a unique pair because it features some of the artworks of the great travelling artist exhibition, White Dunk.

15. Air Jordan Kobe PE Retro Pack 8 Retro 3 – 2016 - 9,273 pounds

This shoe was worn by Kobe back during the 2002 - 2003 NBA season. It was inspired by the Laker's colours and remains one of the most unique sneakers ever made. It goes for a cost of 9,273 pounds making it one of the most expensive shoes around.

The collection of some of the most expensive Nike shoes above may be mind-blowing but also interesting. It is clear that some of the biggest names in sports influence the cost of a pair of shoe, especially when it is named after them. Clearly, one must be loaded to afford some of Nike's most luxurious pairs, even for the limited and rare editions.
