How to Find and Search Particular Storefronts in the Amazon App

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, February 13, 2024

With millions of products to choose from, it's sometimes much easier to search storefronts on the Amazon app than sift through numerous search results. Amazon lets you browse through both influencer and regular storefronts.

In this guide, you'll discover how to search for particular storefronts in the Amazon mobile app. And because it's slightly trickier to find those belonging to influencers, we'll also guide you through how to find someone's Amazon storefront.

What's the Difference Between an Influencer Amazon Storefront and a Regular Storefront?

When you visit a regular Amazon storefront, you'll typically see that manufacturer's items. So, if you visit ASUS' storefront, you can expect to see ASUS computers and the like. This is especially useful if you're on the lookout for one of the best OLED laptops or similar products.

On the other hand, an influencer storefront usually has a collection of products that the individual uses. These are often categorized to make searching for what you want easier, and they can also give you an idea of what that influencer uses for their content creation and more.

Influencers may also share videos and other useful bits of content on their storefronts, which can provide you with inspiration for recipes and whatnot.

How to Find and Search for Regular Amazon Storefronts

If you want to know how to find an Amazon storefront belonging to your favorite manufacturer, we've got you covered. The process is very simple if you follow the steps below.

  • Open the Amazon app on your device and tap the search bar at the top.
  • Type the company's name and the product type you're looking for. In our case, we're going to search for a Canon camera.
  • The first few products will usually be associated with the official store. Choose one of these.
  • Right above the product name at the top, you should see an option titled Visit the [Manufacturer's Name] Store. Tap this.
  • Either scroll down to view the different categories available or select the Categories tab and find what you're looking for.

    How to Find an Influencer's Amazon Storefront

    The process can be more complex when you want to find an individual's Amazon storefront. You have two options in some instances.

    Try Searching for the Influencer's Products

    In some situations, the influencer you're looking for might have released their own products. If you know this is the case, it's a good idea to try searching for these in the Amazon app and follow the same process we mentioned earlier in this guide.

    Search for the Amazon Storefront in Your Web Browser

    When the influencer you're looking for hasn't released a product (or you don't remember the names of those products), your best option is to search in your web browser instead. Follow these steps:

  • Launch your preferred web browser on your mobile device.
  • Search for the influencer and type "amazon storefront" before tapping Go.
  • Select the Amazon storefront for your influencer, which usually is the first result you see on search engines like Google.
  • Search that influencer's storefront. If you find anything that takes your fancy, you can add it to your cart.

    Finding Storefronts on Amazon Is Easy

    You can search for both retailer and influencer storefronts in the Amazon app. The former is better for specific products related to a brand you like, whereas the latter is ideal if you want inspiration and to find something your favorite influencer uses.

    While the process to search for these two storefronts is different in the Amazon app, you can use the steps you've learned today to get your desired results.
