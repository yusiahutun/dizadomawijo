S Club Juniors star Aaron Renfree leaks own full-frontal in reflection fail

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, March 10, 2024

The professional dancer took to Instagram, claiming he was keen for his fans to check out his hotel shower – though decided to pose naked in the process.

Showing off his impressive torso, Aaron posed in his birthday suit using the steamy mirror to protect his modesty.

However, in a seriously epic fail the star failed to realise his manhood was on full view via the reflection in the shower's taps.

Jeez, talk about a rookie error.

Loving the X-rated snap, one fan wrote: "Great pic...if you look closely and in the right spot, you will see something nice."

A second added: "Great reflection LOL."

"Why don't you leak some full frontal?" a third continued.

A fourth joked: "Never been more jealous of a shower in my life."

Aaron initially rose to fame in 2001 after making the mini S Club band alongside Frankie Sandford, Ashley Adams, Daisy Evans, Calvin Goldspink, Stacey McClean, Hannah Richings, and Rochelle Wiseman.

However, after just a couple of years together the teens went their separate ways – with Aaron working hard to carve out a career as a dancer.

Speaking abbot his buff body, Aaron previously said: "At college I was told to buff up because it would help me get better jobs because male dancers get asked to take their tops off all the time – that’s what it comes down to sometimes… Who has the best body.

"It sounds extreme but you have to look camera ready all the time."

He added to Attitude mag: "I can understand why everyone wants to achieve the ‘body beautiful’.

"I don’t see anything wrong in trying to achieve that but I think there is more to life than looking good."
