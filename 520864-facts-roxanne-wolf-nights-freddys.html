10 facts about Roxanne Wolf from Five Nights at Freddy's

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, February 21, 2024

Five Nights At Freddy's is a video game recognized for its anticipated scares. Security Breach, one of the latest installations to the video game, has made it more interesting as it means double the scares. In addition, Security Breach presents a difference in time and introduces fans to several new characters, such as Roxanne Wolf. So, who is she?

Roxanne Wolf is an animatronic from Five Nights are Freddy’s: Security Breach. She starts as one of the secondary antagonists, but her roles shift within the expanded Ruin DLC, making her a minor supporting deuteragonist. Roxy is a recurring minor character in Tales from the Pizza Plex.

Interesting facts about Roxanne Wolf

Roxanne Wolf was one of the four mascots of Freddy Fazbear's Mega Pizzaplex animatronics. She served as the mascot for Roxy's Raceway. Here are top 10 facts about Roxanne Wolf from Five Nights at Freddy's:

1. Her clothing resembles the 1980s

What is Roxanne Wolf's dress code? Roxanne's dressing is reminiscent of the 80s, like the other Glamrocks’ members. She is dressed in:

  • Red crop top, pants and shoulder pads patterned in black star-like shapes.
  • Purple forearms and lower legs, patterned in black tiger print.
  • Black studded wristband on each arm.
  • A studded collar and belt.
  • A hoop and studded earring on each ear.

2. Roxanne Wolf has a great colour palette in her normal state

In her normal appearance, the animatronic is a grey wolf with yellow eyes. She has a wide array of colours. For instance, her hair is silver with green bangs, while her tail is grey with a silver tip. Also, she has silver inner ears, muzzle, fingers, and front torso. Some of the other colours include:

3. A mini-kart hit Roxanne

What happened to Roxanne Wolf? She was badly hit by a mini-kart during the Roxy Raceway, leaving her heavily damaged. After the incident, Gregory takes her eyes and her face is mangled, with the centermost part having a large jagged hole. Her jaw becomes completely loose.

In addition, some huge chunks of her casing go missing while others are broken, exposing the endoskeleton beneath. Her hair is tangled, while oil and dust dirty her body. Her grey tail is almost completely cut off, exposing her underneath endo and wire.

4. She is ruined by events of Security Breach

While the mini-kart badly destroys Roxy, the events of the Security Breach cause her further damage. For instance, a near-complete lack of casing leaves her endoskeleton entirely visible when her shell faces further damage. Some of the other damages she undergoes include;

5. One of her left-hand fingers is missing

Even in the complete ruins, when Cassie wears the Security Mask, Roxanne’s missing body parts reappear in a hologram-like state with a bright green glow.

6. Cassie deactivates Roxanne

Why did Cassie destroy Roxy? Cassie forces herself to deactivate Roxanne after Gregory tells her that she is the last security node to finally save him. At this point, players are forced since Roxanne is the last Security Node keeping Gregory trapped.

After Cassie destroys her favourite animatronic, she is seen mourning her destruction and even apologizes to the powered-down Roxanne. She even cries over the encounter while walking into the final stage.

7. She has a superiority complex

What does Roxanne Wolf say to herself? She tells herself that she is the best. At the beginning of the game, Roxy is displayed as a character who is full of herself. She is seen complimenting herself on various occasions, such as when standing in front of the mirror.

Thanks to her artificial intelligence being advanced enough to have human-level intelligence, Glamrock Roxy has also developed self-esteem issues. When she mocks Gregory, there is a likelihood that she is projecting her insecurities.

8. Roxanne Wolf has a twin sister

Who is Roxanne Wolf's sister? Her sister is blue Roxanne. She comes to light in a short titled Roxanne’s Twin Sister, where Gregory and Freddy encounter two Roxannes.

Gregory and Freddy are determined to find who the real one among them is. After several attempts, they distinguish the two, with the imposter crashing into a wall and shattering like glass.

9. She is falling for Glamrock Foxy

Who does Roxanne love? Roxy loves Glamrock Foxy. He is a character first seen in the Security Breach: Ruin DLC during Roxy's Raceway. Even though he was an animatronic, he wasn't one of the main stage members but another daycare attendant.

When Foxy goes to the Mega Pizza plex as a Glamrock Animatronic, he finds a new home and four new friends, including Roxy. He immediately falls head over heels in love with Roxanne. On the other hand, Roxy is also starting to slowly fall in love with him.

10. She was rebuilt with Foxy’s parts

Why did Glamrock Foxy turn into Roxanne Wolf? Glamrock Foxy turned into Roxanne Wolf because they used Foxy’s parts. Foxy had already been scrapped when all the Pizza Plex animatronics were created. Some of his parts were used in Roxy's creation.

11. Roxanne operated as a blind animatronic

What happened to Roxanne’s eyes? Gregory took out her eyes and gave them to Freddy. Roxie was rendered unconscious after Gregory tricked Cassie into deactivating her. In her powered-down state, he took out her eyes.

Roxie is not weighed down by her blindness. Instead, she continues to operate throughout the remaining part of the video game as a blind animatronic. However, her state makes her emotionally unstable, as displayed in her harsh threats and frequent breakdowns.
