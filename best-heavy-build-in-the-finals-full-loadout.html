Best Heavy Build in The Finals: Full Loadout

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, March 24, 2024

The best Heavy build in The Finals involves creating a strong and durable frontline unit. Here’s the best loadout available.

Overall, the best Heavy build uses the SA1216 weapon, the RPG-7, Frag and Pyro Grenade Gadgets, and the Charge ‘N’ Slam specialization.

Let’s go through why this is the best loadout and also propose some alternatives in terms of weapons and specialization.

Best Heavy Build Weapon in The Finals

The best weapon in The Finals for the Heavy build is the SA1216 or the Sledgehammer. The former is an auto shotgun ideal for close-range fights, while the latter is a hard-hitting melee weapon.

With gadgets and specialization that will make it easier to get close to enemies, these weapons will wreak havoc, especially against Light builds.

Best Heavy Gadgets

The best Heavy gadgets are the following:

  • RPG-7: One of the best gadgets in The Finals, the RPG-7 can one-shot Light builds and deal a lot of damage to Medium ones. It’s ideal since it allows you to be a huge threat even from afar.
  • Frag Grenade: The best grenade in the game as it comes with two uses and is ideal for clearing areas.
  • Pyro Grenade: Great utility nade that can be used to clear corners or to block doorways and temporarily stop enemies from pushing.

With these gadgets, you’ll be able to control close-quarters areas next to cash-out points and make enemies afraid of pushing and getting close.

Best Heavy Specialization

In terms of specialization, you have two options. The Charge ‘N’ Slam is your best bet for solo/uncoordinated gameplay, while the Mesh Shield is ideal if you’re playing with your team.

The former allows you to charge through walls, get close to enemies, and secure areas more easily. The latter allows your teammates to stand behind you and shoot without being exposed.

If you’re planning to work closely together with your teammates, Mesh Shield is amazing. However, if you’re playing with randoms who push alone, you’ll get more value from Charge ‘N’ Slam.

Wrapping up the Best Heavy Build

In conclusion, the best Heavy build in The Finals is a strong frontline unit that can take down enemies quickly if they get close with the Sledgehammer or SA1216 shotgun.

Additionally, the Charge ‘N’ Slam spec allows them to charge in and get close to enemies, while the RPG-7 is an ideal option for one-shotting any Light enemy. Lastly, the Frag and Pyro Grenades are extremely versatile, and you can never go wrong with them.
