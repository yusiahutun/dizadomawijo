Emily Ratajkowski's toned abs peek out of mesh ripped mini dress at star-studded bash

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, June 7, 2024

Emily Ratajkowski is back to stun the Internet in a new outfit as she gets snapped returning from the Vogue World event.

Emily Ratajkowski has been on top of her fashion game in 2023 as she has donned a number of scintillating outfits this year carving her place in the it-girl fashion history just for herself. The celebrity‘s latest mesh look included a peak of her toned abs through numerous holes in the dress.

Emily Ratajkowski’s toned abs peek out of mesh ripped mini dress

Emily Ratajkowski knows how to flaunt her toned physique as she gets snapped wearing an all-black mesh dress with numerous holes that reveals plenty of her skin.

The model was accompanied by British fashion model Adwoa Aboah and American model Paloma Elsesser who attended the Vogue World: London 2023 after-party at George Club in Mayfair on September 14.

Emily Ratajkowski’s brunette locks look iconic

From blond hair to red dye, Emily has been comfortable experimenting with her hair but her current brunette locks seem iconic as it is.

After dying her hair a mesmerizing ginger shade in mid-July, the 32-year-old introduced a new chocolate brown look in August end on her Instagram and fans have been praising her current look calling it ‘iconic.

Vogue World after party sees Ashley Graham and Maisie Williams

Vogue World After Part in London saw a huge number of stars turning up for the event along with Emily Ratajkowski. The star-studded guest list included Ashley Graham, Maisie Williams, Jenna Coleman, Rita Ora, and Jodie Turner Smith.

As per reports, Stormzy, Claire Foy, Tom Daley, Daisy Edgar Jones, and Ashley Walters also attended the glam night.
