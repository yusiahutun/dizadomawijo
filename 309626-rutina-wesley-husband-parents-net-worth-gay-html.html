Rutina Wesley biography

Actress Wesley was born in 1978 in Las Vegas, Nevada. She studied at the Las Vegas Academy of International Studies, Visual Arts and Performing. She also studied dance at the West Las Vegas Arts Center and Simba Studios.

While in high school, Wesley missed numerous auditions to join top-notch training colleges. Nonetheless, she earned a spot in the University of Evansville at Indiana to pursue a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Theatre Performance.

In 2001, she graduated from the university. Her grandmother then suggested that she goes back to school to study nursing. However, determined Wesley insisted on following her artistic passion. She joined the Julliard School and graduated later in 2015. During her stay in the college, she met and befriended Nelsan Ellis who would then in the future turn to be her co-star.
