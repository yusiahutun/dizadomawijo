How did Tabitha Brown become famous?

Towards the end of 2007, she started running out of disability funds and thus resolved to become an Uber driver in October 2017 to make ends meet. In December 2017, she shared a Whole Foods Market vegan BLT sandwich review.

The clip went viral, after which the company hired her as their brand ambassador to travel the country. In early March 2020, she created a TikTok account where she shared different content, such as:

  • Vegan recipes
  • Cooking tips
  • Family moments
  • Encouraging advice

In five weeks, her TikTok account had amassed over 2 million followers. In 2018, she started broadcasting live cooking shows and product reviews on her Facebook account. Towards the end of April 2020, she was signed to Creative Artists Agency.

In June of the same year, she started hosting All Love on Ellen Digital Network. In 2021, she hosted the YouTube Original children's show titled Tab Time.
