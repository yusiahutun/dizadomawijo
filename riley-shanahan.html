Riley Shanahan - Bio, Age, Height, Net Worth, Facts, Nationality

Table of Biography

Riley Shanahan is a famous actor from the United States. Riley Shanahan is widely known for playing Robotman in the superhero comedy-drama television series Doom Patrol.

Early Life

Riley Shanahan was born in Sacramento, California, United States on 3 December 1991. Shanahan is 32 years old with the zodiac sign Sagittarius.

He holds an American nationality and follows the Christian nationality. Besides that, Shanahan has not shared any information regarding his parents or siblings.

Education

Speaking of his educational background, he went to Jesuit High School Sacramento and earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in acting from the Cornish College of the Arts.

Career and Professional Life

Talking about his career, The comedy-drama short film Four Walls from 2014, which Shanahan also co-wrote, marked his acting debut. Then he played the part of Dex Gamble in the 2016 comedy The Gender Card Flip.

After that, he costarred with Alicia Vikander in the 2019 short film I Am Easy to Find as a young father. The short film, which was written and directed by Mike Mills, was eventually included in the music video for the same-titled album by the rock band The National in May 2019.

Caption: Riley Shanahan in the superhero comedy-drama television series Doom Patrol source: X.

Shanahan received his breakthrough part as Robotman on Doom Patrol that same year. In 2020, he also had an appearance in the same role in DC’s Legends of Tomorrow’s Season 5 Episode 3 titled “Miss Me, Kiss Me, Love Me.”

He co-wrote, co-produced, and starred in the comedy short Breakdown in 2020. Additionally, he served as executive producer of the short film You Should Get Out More from 2021.
