Shop the Jo Malone 2023 beauty advent calendar now

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, March 19, 2024

What's the first sign that Christmas is coming? Among the serious beauty fans at least, it's the flurry of beauty advent calendars that begin to dominate the shelves. And while there may be dozens vying for your attention this year, Jo Malone London's chic creation remains one of the most coveted.

An annual sell-out without fail, Jo Malone's beauty advent calendar is the pinnacle of the fragrance brand's festive collection. This year's offering is chic yet fun, combining that instantly recognisable cream and black packaging with candy-cane stripes and gingerbread-inspired details.

So, what exactly is in store? You can expect a welcome mix of candles, bodycare and of course, those globally adored colognes in fragrances both old and new.

The bright and zingy Lime, Basil & Mandarin mini candle is a particular highlight, as is the indulgent Velvet Rose & Oud cologne. But perhaps best of all is the inclusion of a full-sized 30ml fragrance, which can be unwrapped on December 25. Trust us: this year, it's a crowd-pleasing classic.
