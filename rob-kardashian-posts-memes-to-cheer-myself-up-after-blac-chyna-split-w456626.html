Rob Kardashian Posts Memes 'to Cheer Myself Up' After Blac Chyna Split

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, February 9, 2024

What’s a man to do? Rob Kardashian posted fan-made memes of himself on Saturday, December 17, after announcing that his fiancée, Blac Chyna, had left him and taken their 1-month-old daughter, Dream, too. Watch the video above to see what Kardashian documented on social media, after Chyna left their house.

The first meme the reality star shared to Instagram was a screenshot of Will Smith at the end of the Fresh Prince of Bel-Air series, standing alone and looking forlorn in the middle of an empty room. “Rob Kardashian currently at his house, while Blac Chyna is gone,” the meme reads.

“😩 but she took the baby also and she took my eggos and my chips and my sweet baby rays BBQ sauce,” he captioned the post. A second meme he shared included his face superimposed on Macaulay Culkin’s on the cover of Home Alone, with Chyna’s face Photoshopped in behind him. “A FAMILY COMEDY WITHOUT THE FAMILY 😩,” he captioned the image.

https://www.instagram.com/p/BOJPmeJADbj/

After fans began to question whether Chyna, 28, had really left Kardashian, 29, given his seemingly lighthearted take on the news, the Arthur George designer clarified that he was reposting memes in an effort to cheer himself up.

“People are tagging me in these memes and just cuz I’m posting them doesn’t mean this wasn’t all serious,” he wrote. “This is all very real and serious to me and I’m trying to cheer myself up with these and they are making me laugh. So relax. This wasn’t fake or some publicity stunt. Trust me. My one month old beautiful baby girl got taken from me along with my wife who left me. Been upset for days so relax. This is all very real in my life. I don’t play when it comes to my woman and my baby.”

https://www.instagram.com/p/BOJP4y-AoNw/

Kardashian posted one last meme, which showed the Lashed Bar founder’s face superimposed on the Grinch’s face in an ad for How the Grinch Stole Christmas, with “Grinch” crossed out and “Chyna” emblazoned across it.

As Us Weekly previously reported, a series of explosive events took place on Saturday, December 17. First, Chyna’s Instagram account was hacked and littered with posts of alleged direct messages detailing her plans to trademark her Kardashian name after the pair gets married next July, as well as alleged conversations between her and Young Thug and Jaden Smith.

https://www.instagram.com/p/BOJXStjAfI1/

Then, Kardashian shared Snapchat videos of himself in a house stripped bare of his daughter Dream’s nursery furniture. In the clips, he told followers that Chyna had left him and taken Dream with her. “I’ve got the Christmas tree all set up for Chyna and the baby but they left me,” he said in one of the videos.

In response, Chyna took to a new Instagram account (since deleted) to tell her side of the story. “I’M DONE! This entire year I have done nothing but help Rob!” she ranted. “It’s so SAD & PATHETIC how low he’d stoop to cover up HIS PERSONAL ISSUES! Rob is mentally ill & refuses to seek help! He self medicates which makes it worse. I have done nothing but help & loved him from the beginning! It’s Chy here I got my own!!!”
