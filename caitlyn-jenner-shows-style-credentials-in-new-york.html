Caitlyn Jenner shows style credentials on day out in New York

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, September 19, 2024

    All eyes were on Caitlyn Jenner as she stepped out in three fashionable outfits over the course of a busy day in New York on Tuesday. The 65-year-old appeared more confident than ever as she continued to experiment with her style during a day out in the Big Apple.

    The star's day began with a visit to costume designer Patricia Field's shop. Caitlyn looked chic in a leopard print Diane Von Furstenberg wrap dress paired with black slingback heels and her long hair worn down in loose waves.

    Caitlyn Jenner wore a leopard print wrap dress to visit Patricia Field's shop

    She had clearly been tempted by the designer duds on sale - as she left the store wearing a completely different ensemble. The second outfit of the day was in complete contrast to the sophisticated wrap dresses and blouses she has been wearing of late. Instead the former Olympian had fun wearing a colourful sequin mini skirt and black sequin top which she covered up with a fitted black jacket.

    Caitlyn's packed day didn't end there though; she later enjoyed an evening at the theatre, catching a performance of on Broadway with her friend, transgender actress Candis Cayne.

    Caitlyn left the shop in a colourful sequin mini skirt

    Choosing to wear another DVF wrap dress, Caitlyn looked glamorous in the navy blue lace number which she teamed with her trusty black heels.

    The high-profile outing follows a series of public appearances in New York in recent days, showing Caitlyn's increased confidence in her new identity.
