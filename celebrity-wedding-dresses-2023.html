The most memorable celebrity wedding dresses of 2023

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, February 23, 2024
1

Sofia Richie Grainge in Chanel

2

Georgia Fowler in Vivienne Westwood and Danielle Frankel

Georgia Fowler is sure to be on every upcoming bride's moodboard thanks to her beautiful Vivienne Westwood gown. The model wore a silk, corseted dress – a silhouette that is instantly recognisable as a Vivienne Westwood signature – and a long, floor-length veil. Fowler later swapped into a Danielle Frankel design as they changed locations on her big day.

3

Taylor Hill in Etro

Taylor Hill married Daniel Fryer wearing a romantic Etro gown, set against the backdrop of the Colorado mountains. Her off-the-shoulder dress was a perfect match for the natural setting, with its delicate sheer material and gently ruffled edges.

4

Barbara Palvin in Vivenne Westwood

Hungarian model Barbara Palvin married actor Dylan Sprouse in her home country, wearing a timeless gown by Vivienne Westwood, a diamond choker by Tiffany & Co and a pair of white opera gloves. In Hungary, it is tradition for the bride to wear a red dress after midnight – or menyecske ruha – so she changed out of her ivory gown in favour of a red mini-dress by Philosophy di Lorenzo Serafini.

5

Michelle Yeoh in Schiaparelli

After taking home the Leading Actress Oscar, Michelle Yeoh continued her celebratory year by marrying her long-term partner Jean Todt, to whom she'd been engaged for 19 years. The bride wore a soft gold Schiaparelli design – aptly named the 'Face of Happiness' dress – which featured a lace base, worn underneath a whimsical embroidered corset.

6

Naomi Watts in Oscar de la Renta

7

Beanie Feldstein in Gucci

Beanie Feldstein and Bonnie-Chance Roberts got married in May in the Hudson Valley, both wearing Gucci. Feldstein opted for this bespoke lace gown with a long train and shimmering bow-tie detail, which she praised for feeling simultaneously vintage and remarkably modern. "It almost feels like they took a dress from a hundred years ago and restored it," she wrote on Instagram.

8

Amelia Spencer in Atelier Versace and Swarovski

Lady Amelia Spencer – daughter of the Earl of Spencer and the model Victoria Aitken – married lifestyle coach Greg Mallett in South Africa back in March. Both the bride and groom wore Atelier Versace for their big day; Spencer's floral appliqué gown featured a two-and-a-half-metre train, semi-sheer bodice and Swarovski crystals.

9

Simone Biles in Galia Lahav

American gymnast Simone Biles wore not one but four wedding dresses on her special day, marrying her longtime love Jonathan Owens in Cabo. She began the celebrations in a Galia Lahav mini-dress with shimmering embellishments, then walked down the aisle in a 3D floral lace design, before changing into the brand's Maya mermaid gown for the reception. Finally, she danced the night away in a long embellished dress by the bridal brand.

10

Vanessa Hudgens in Vera Wang

Vanessa Hudgens and Cole Tucker closed out the year with a December wedding in the Mexican jungle. The bride wore a sleek Vera Wang slip dress with a cowl neckline, and a dramatic tulle veil.
