Everything to know about Lauren London

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, June 23, 2024

STILL recovering from the tragic death of late rapper Nipsey Hussle, long-time girlfriend Lauren London continues to strive for greatness in his honor.

The American actress has since starred in a new Netflix comedy, You People, and even has her own fashion line with Puma.

Who is Lauren London?

Lauren London is an actress, model, and TV personality.

She was born on December 5, 1984, in Los Angeles, California.

Lauren is the mother of two sons.

In 2009, Lauren welcomed her first son, Kameron Carter, with another famous rapper, Lil Wayne.

She shares her second child with the late rapper Nipsey Hussle.

Their child, Kross Ermias Asghedom, was born in 2016.

Both of her sons are often pictured on her Instagram, where she has more than ten million followers.

How long were Lauren London and Nipsey Hussle together?

The former couple began dating in 2013, three years before they welcomed their son together, Essence reports.

In a 2019 interview with GQ Magazine, Lauren told the outlet she sought out Nipsey to buy a few copies of his limited edition mixtape Crenshaw.

She said: "I did not slide into his DMs.

"My homeboy did."

Lauren and Nipsey were often spotted together in Los Angeles, especially sitting courtside at NBA games.

The famous couple were together until the rapper's tragic death on March 31, 2019.

Nipsey was shot and killed at the age of 33.

Where is Lauren London now?

Lauren has been busy working on her latest collaboration with the athletic brand Puma.

Her collection is called LA Love and features clothing and footwear inspired by her hometown.

It also features pieces inspired by her late beau.

In addition to her work with Puma, Lauren starred in the 2023 Netflix original movie You People.

However, four years after Nipsey's death, Lauren admitted she is still healing from her devastating loss.

In November 2022, she told E! News: "It's something that will continue forever.

"Healing is not like a straight arrow - It goes up and down, so I don't think that I am done with anything.

"In my life now, anything that I do I want it to have purpose and I want it to be intentional.

"I hope that he would be proud that I am taking a chance on myself."

Is Lauren London on social media?

Lauren has a strong presence on social media and engages regularly with fans through personal updates and private messages.

On her Instagram she has more than ten million followers.
