Kaia Gerber flaunts her slim figure in a black sports bra and leggings while out in Los Angeles

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Kaia Gerber showed off her toned frame on Monday as she stepped out in Los Angeles.

The 22-year-old model - whose mom is Cindy Crawford - was dressed in a black bra with thin straps and matching skintight leggings.

Kaia's lustrous brown locks were parted down the center as they flowed down her back.

She carried a white canvas tote bag over her shoulder that read 'still here' in capital black letters.

The American Horror Story star finished her athleisure outfit with white socks and a pair of white and silver Asics sneakers.

The star added a touch of glamour as she covered her doe eyes in a pair of oval, brown sunglasses.

She didn't appear to have any makeup on her face as she showed off a clear complexion.

In addition to balancing her work as a runway regular and budding actress, Kaia is girlfriend to actor Austin Butler, 32.

The couple is often spotted out and about together, and they were recently seen enjoying each other's company at a farmer's market.

The superstars were first linked shortly after Kaia broke things off with her previous boyfriend, Euphoria star Jacob Elordi in 2021.

Kaia and Austin made their first red carpet debut at a W Magazine party in Los Angeles in March 2022.

Since then, they've appeared to make it a priority to spend quality time together, despite their demanding schedules.
