Ina Balin’s Bio, Wiki

Ina Balin was born as Ina Rosenberg on 12th November 1937 in Brooklyn, New York City, New York, the United States. Her father, Sam Rosenberg was a dancer, singer, and comedian. Whereas, her mother was also a dancer who first married at the age of 15. Ina’s father was the third husband of her mother.

Ina grew up along with her brother, Richard Balin. When both of them were growing, their parents divorced. Their mother was going to marry for the fourth time. Later, Harold Balin adopted both of the siblings. Balin had graduated from high school at the age of 15. Also, she spent five years at a boarding school in Pennsylvania.

Ina Balin’s Career

Taking a dream of getting established in the film industry, Ina Balin made her first appearance on TV through The Perry Como Show. After that, she made a guest appearance in the American TV series, Wonder Woman. Following, she guest-starred in several TV series such as Adventures in Paradise, The Lieutenant, Battlestar Galactica, It Takes a Thief, and so on.

After grabbing the attention of several people in the movie industry, Ina started getting opportunities. She landed her first film role in 1959 in The Black Orchid. In 1960, she appeared in the TV film, From The Terrace. Her other film credits are such as The Comancheros (1961), The Patsy (1964), Charro! (1969), Desperate Mission (1969), The Immigrants (1978), Vasectomy: A Delicate Matter (1986) and so on.

Besides, Ina had also made her appearance in the 1969 TV movie, The Lonely Profession alongside Joseph Cotten, Fernando Lamas, and Dean Jagger.

Ina Balin’s Personal Life: Married, Children

Ina Balin had never married to anyone. She lived a single life until her death. However, she had adopted three children during the fall of Saigon in 1975. The children named Nguyet Baty, Ba-Nhi Mai, and Kim Thuy. Also, Ina had played in a movie based on her experiences titled The Children of An Lac.

During the time, Balin got identified with Christy Marx, an American screenwriter, author, game designer. At that time, Marx used to work for various TV programs. Later, she wrote the story of an American animated TV series, Jem where she put a character of Ba Nee who is based on Balin’s adopted daughter, Ba-Nhi.

In addition, two of her adopted kids Ba-Nhi Mai and Kim Thuy were raised by Ted Ashley, a Hollywood talent agent and his wife, Page Cuddy. Well, Balin was so active in humanitarian activities. She used to help whoever needed to be helped.

Besides, Balin had never been a part of any rumors or controversies.

What was the cause of Ina Balin’s Death? Her Age

Ina Balin died at the age of 52. The reason for her death was chronic lung disease. She used to smoke a lot which caused pulmonary hypertension or high blood pressure of lungs in her body. As per the doctors, she needed to have a lung transplant very immediately. However, she died on 20th June 1990.
