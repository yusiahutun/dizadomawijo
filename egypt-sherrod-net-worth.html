What Is HGTV Star Egypt Sherrod's Net Worth?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, February 25, 2024

If you know HGTV, then you've probably heard of Egypt Sherrod. From Married to Real Estate to Property Virgins to Flipping Virgins, Sherrod has hosted her fair share of shows on the network, and undoubtably amassed a considerable fortune along the way. Sherrod is often seen alongside her husband Mike Jackson and her two daughters Harper and Kendall. Season 3 of Married to Real Estate, starring Sherrod and Jackson, kicked off on December 28, 2023, with 11 more episodes to come in 2024. With Egypt's real estate expertise and Mike's knack for building and woodworking, the couple works together to help families move into their dream neighborhoods in Atlanta, GA. The new series marked Sherrod's return to HGTV after a brief hiatus.

Aside from her HGTV hosting responsibilities, Sherrod keeps busy with numerous other projects and business ventures. She is one of the hosts for the WVEE radio station in Atlanta, a realtor, an author, and a mother. Her book Keep Calm... It's Just Real Estate: Your No-Stress Guide to Buying a Home made its way to becoming a best-seller, increasing her fortune even more. She is also the founder of a nonprofit called Egypt Cares Family Foundation, which provides resources to families promoting financial literacy, higher education, and family communication. Of course, with her multiple business ventures and growing number of fans on social media and beyond, there are plenty of questions swirling around about the entrepreneur. One question in particular we all want to know is: What is Egypt Sherrod's net worth?

What is Egypt Sherrod's Net Worth in 2024?

Currently, Celebrity Net Worth estimates Egypt Sherrod's net worth at a whopping $6 million. This number reflects a combination of earning from her various business ventures over the course of her career (and she's only 47!). Since marrying Mike Jackson in 2010, the couple has only increased their collective net worth as a team and with Married to Real Estate.

You can catch more of Egypt and Mike on season 3 of Married to Real Estate airing now on HGTV and streaming on MAX and Discovery+. "The stakes are higher, the projects are bigger, and the funny is even funnier!," Sherrod teased of the new season in an interview with House Beautiful.
