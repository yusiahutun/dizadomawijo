Melissa George and Jean-David Blanc welcome second son Solal Samuel Glenn

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, April 22, 2024

The Slap star Melissa George and boyfriend Jean-David Blanc welcomed their second son.

And the 39-year-old took to Twitter to celebrate the big news, announcing the arrival of Solal Samuel Glenn Blanc.

'I love you baby boy,' the happy mother-of-two gushed, while also sharing the healthy baby arrived at 4.2 kilo on on November 3.

On hold: The debut of Melissa's upcoming new NBC series - Heartbreaker, in which she stars as transplant surgeon Alex Panttiere - was delayed due to her pregnancy
