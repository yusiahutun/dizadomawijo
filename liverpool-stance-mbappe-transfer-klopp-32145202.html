Liverpool stance on Kylian Mbappe transfer as Jurgen Klopp breaks silence on PSG star's exit

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Liverpool will not enter the race to sign Kylian Mbappe this summer.

The France forward is set to leave PSG with Real Madrid the red-hot favourites to land one of the world’s best players. Mbappe, 25, is likely to demand a huge signing on fee as well as eye-watering wages to get his signature.

Kop boss Jurgen Klopp won’t be at Anfield beyond the end of the season so will have no say if the club moves for him but it is understood that the Reds won’t pursue the forward.

Klopp was asked if many top clubs will be exploring a deal for him with Real the heavy favourites. He said: “Obviously, I’m not involved in that, but I can tell you I’d be surprised if all the top clubs were. The top clubs I know, for most of them it will be tricky - wages, signing-on fee.”
