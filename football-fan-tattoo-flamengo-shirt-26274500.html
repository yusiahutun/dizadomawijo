Football fan spends one year getting beloved teams shirt tattooed on his entire torso

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Football fans often debate over who loves their club more, or who is the more passionate supporter, but there are no questions being asked regarding Brazilian side Flamengo.

It's common that die-hard fans get tattoo's associated with the club that they support, with badges, slogans or even trophies typically the common theme.

However, one man in Brazil has taken his love for the club that one step further, and tattooed the Flamengo shirt across his entire torso to ensure that he is wearing the colours of his team for life.

In this incredibly dedicated and bizarre stunt, the fan spent a whole year completing his masterpiece.

Mauricio dos Anjos is now permanently wearing the Flamengo shirt after getting their famous red and black colours inked on his torso, with the no.10 etched on his back.

According to Vice, the tattoo took a whole year, as 32 sessions were needed which resulted in 90 hours of work being done, and it didn't even cost a penny as the artist was happy doing it for exposure.

Dos Anjos explained his decision, as he said: "I always wanted to have a Flamengo tattoo, ever since I became a fan as a child.

"I got a vulture [the team mascot] with the Flamengo shield on my biceps. Last year, I decided to get a tattoo of the jersey, which is something I'd actually been wanting to get for a while.

"But I thought it would be too expensive - between R$10,000 (£2,000) and R$15,000 (£3,000). So I chose to get something smaller, that covered only part of my shoulder and chest.

"The tattoo artist asked for R$800 (£165), but as we chatted he told me he'd do the entire jersey at no extra cost, just for the exposure."

Dos Anjos has insisted that he has no regrets following his decision, and the Brazilian will never have to fork out around £40 for another Flamengo shirt ever again.
