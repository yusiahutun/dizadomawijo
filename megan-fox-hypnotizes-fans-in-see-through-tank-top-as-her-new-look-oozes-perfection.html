Megan Fox 'hypnotizes' fans in see-through tank top as her new look oozes 'perfection'

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, May 5, 2024

Megan Fox has finally debuted her fiery red bob on Instagram, to promote her new book, and fans cannot get enough.

The Hollywood star first showed off her new look back in September when she was spotted out with her partner, Machine Gun Kelly. Although Instagram got a delayed debut, it was worth the wait.

Megan Fox debuts ‘stunning’ red hair on Instagram

Taking to her 21.6 million Instagram followers, the Jennifer’s Body star posted a number of red-haired selfies to promote her new poetry book, Pretty Boys Are Poisonous, out now.

To celebrate the launch, the star took to NYC to discuss what had inspired the release in front of an audience.

As always, she killed it with the fashion, wearing an all brown ensemble, consisting of a very see-through sheer tank top and chic leather skirt.

Finishing off the stylish look, Megan Fox sported some PVC knee-high boots. As well as glossy boots, her lips were also glossed to the max, as she pouted in her Insta pic.

Sticking with the matching theme, her red hair and nails went hand in hand.

In the caption, she penned: “an author [stick out tongue emoji] my book is out link in bio [broken heart, heart emoji]”

“I swear any look, any hair color, etc is perfection on you,” wrote one fan.

“SO PROUD OF YOU!!!” exclaimed another.

Another said: “These pics have hypnotized me”

One fan penned: “You keep inspiring me to be a more authentic me on the outside not just inside. You’re a powerful woman. A Warrior.”

The star wants to ‘inspire others’

Although the publication was released this week, fans have been patiently waiting since August when she first announced her book release.

Giving fans an insight into why she wrote the poems, at the time, she penned:

“These poems were written in an attempt to excise the illness that had taken root in me because of my silence. I’ve spent my entire life keeping the secrets of men, by body aches from carrying the weight of their sins.”

“My freedom lives in these pages and I hope that my words can inspire others to take back their happiness and their identity by using their voice to illuminate what’s being buried, but not forgotten in the darkness.”

Commenting on the post, her fiance, MGK wrote: “proud of you,” alongside a fire emoji.
