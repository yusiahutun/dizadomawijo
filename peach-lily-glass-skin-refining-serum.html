If you haven’t heard of the ‘glass skin’ trend, it essentially translates to super smooth, poreless skin—you know, like glass. The K-Beauty term has been steadily gaining popularity for several years now, and Peach & Lily is one of the few to credit for it; the brand’s bestselling Glass Skin Serum aims to help users achieve the coveted, glass-like complexion.

The key to getting glass skin is by keeping the face hydrated, inflammation-free and protected against radical damage. The lightweight serum will do just that—a “revolutionary cocktail” of niacinamide, East Asian mountain yam, Centella Asiatica, peach extract, peptides and hyaluronic acid, the main ingredients visibly brighten and firm the skin for a “crystal-clear” appearance.

The brand tested over two hundred formulas (including through face tests on over forty cosmetic chemists) to see if it could craft a serum that lived up to its potential name. Peach & Lily wanted a product that resulted in “The kind of skin that is so luminous, with pores so smooth, and a clarity of skin so crystal-like that it looks like a beaming slab of pristine glass,” per the brand’s website.

In addition, the serum has gone through the consumer study ring: 100 percent of test subjects said the serum is suitable for sensitive skin, 96 percent saw smoother and softer skin, 96 percent witnessed increased hydration and lastly, 92 percent noticed brighter, more luminous skin with continued use.
