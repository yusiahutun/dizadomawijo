Kendall Jenner Wore a Nude Slip Dress with Cowboy Boots

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, April 4, 2024

Kendall Jenner has a knack for pulling off literally anything â€” to be fair that is technically the job description for model, but still. Confirming this statement to be true, Jenner and her NBA player boyfriend Devin Booker had a cute date night at Nobu in Malibu, and for the occasion, she put together two items from her closet that fall on opposite ends of the fashion spectrum.

In photographs obtained by Daily Mail, the supermodel paired a feminine nude silk slip dress with an unexpected pair of black leather cowboy boots. The edgy combo actually worked flawlessly despite the juxtaposition in style. The Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum also accessorized with an ivory leather shoulder bag that had a banana clip attached to the strap.

Booker, for his part, wore a red and white striped long-sleeve shirt, black pants, and a face mask.

The happy couple recently returned from their romantic European getaway (them and literally every other Hollywood couple, included), where they vacationed and sailed around Positano, Nerano, Ponza, and Sardinia in Italy. They even posed for an adorable and rare photo together one night out in Capri.

A source recently told Entertainment Tonight that while the couple is getting serious and are very much committed to each other, they're not rushing into anything. "They're not putting any sort of pressure on things for the future," the source said. "They realize they're both still young and have big futures ahead of them. Right now they're just enjoying their time together and taking things as they come."

The source added, "Devin is sweet, smart, fun and totally not a player. He is very respectful and was raised with very good values. Kendall finds that to be a very attractive quality."
