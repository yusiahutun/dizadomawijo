The popular serum is a cult-favorite for good reason: It's said to be 62 times more powerful than vitamin C, which is the gold-standard antioxidant for brightening skin. Plus, it won’t sensitize skin, which is often the case with vitamin C. Caudalie is well-known for routine staples inspired by the antioxidant properties in grapes, so it’s no surprise that this celeb-favorite serum uses skin-boosting viniferine as its star ingredient. Viniferine is harnessed from the sap of grapevine stalks, and stops visible dark spots in their tracks while preventing new ones from forming. The serum also nicely features olive squalane to keep your skin plump and hydrated.

As a beauty editor whose darks spots seem to "magically" appear every summer like clockwork, I personally rely on the Vinoperfect serum for a boost of radiance. The first time I applied it, I noticed an immediate glow, and just a few weeks later (with consistent use), my skin's tone and texture only continued to improve—which is why it's now one of my holy-grail favorites.

If you’re looking for a year-round glow in a bottle, Amazon Prime Day is the perfect time to snag the editor- and celebrity-approved Caudalie Vinoperfect Radiance Dark Spot Serum at a fraction of the price.
