Frank Productions Announces The Launch Of FPC Live

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, May 2, 2024

MADISON, WI (CelebrityAccess) — Just two week after announcing that they’d been acquired by Live Nation, Frank Productions, Inc. announces the creation of FPC Live in Madison, Wisconsin.

This new company comes after the recent merger of Frank Productions Concerts and Majestic Live and spells the retirement of both of those brands, as well as True Endeavors, which has been part of FPC for more than five years.

Majestic Live co-owners Scott Leslie and Matt Gerding along with Frank Productions Concerts President Charlie Goldstone as Presidents of the new venture. FPC Live will continue to operate under the Frank Productions aegis and its executive suite– co-COOs Larry and Fred Frank, CFO Marla Frank, and CEO Joel Plant.

FPC Live will also continue its partnership with parent company Frank Production’s other ventures, including NS2 in Nashville, TN and CMoore Live in Boise, ID.

“Frank Productions has more than 50 years of history in the concert industry and we are simultaneously honoring the generations of experience and creating a new, broader team that reflects our vision for the future of live music,” says Goldstone. “FPC Live will continue the same artist-driven, partnership-based approach that its predecessor companies have championed from club to stadium shows and build upon that for the next generation of our company.”

Upcoming tours and concerts from FPL include Avenged Sevenfold w/ Breaking Benjamin’s tour, which kicks off in February.
