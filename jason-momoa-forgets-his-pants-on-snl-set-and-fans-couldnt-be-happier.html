Jason Momoa forgets his pants on SNL set and fans 'couldn't be happier'

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, May 7, 2024

Jason Momoa is returning to SNL on November 18 but this time, he’s ‘pantless’.

SNL viewers hail Jason Momoa’s guest appearances on the show as some of their favorites from the live comedy series. The movie star made his debut on Saturday Night Live back in December 2018. Accompanied by music legends Mumford & Sons and surprise guest Robert De Niro, Jason’s skit impressed fans. He returned to the TV show again on an October 2019 episode during Chance the Rapper’s hosting stint as a surprise guest.

Jason Momoa is thrilled to be back on SNL. But his excitement might have caused him to forget one key element of his wardrobe! The 44-year-old Hollywood sensation arrives in the promo video donning a hat, a chic neck piece that might remind viewers of Game of Thrones, a graphic tee, and a leather jacket.

But co-host Tate McRae discovers that Jason is not wearing his pants.

The Dune star quickly apologizes to the celebrity for appearing pantless. He smiles: “Sorry, when I am excited, I forget my pants.” Later when Tate hurries to arrange pants for the star, he rushes off from the scene shouting “not a chance.”

Jason makes viewers happier than ever

Jason’s pantless stint is already building excitement prior to its release on November 18. One fan applauded the star’s energy on set, writing on X (Twitter): “Love his enthusiasm!”

Another user gushed over Jason Momoa’s acting saying: “YESSSS!! He had the best skits, last time he was on (Elf on the shelf, Rudolph)!”

Jason Momoa is iconic on SNL says a third fan adding: “Him being pantless is making me happier than ever!”

“It’ll be another rockin’ night,” a fourth fan comments on SNL’s official Instagram post.

Saturday Night Live is on NBC every Saturday at 11:30/10:30. The show will be available for streaming the next day on Peacock.
