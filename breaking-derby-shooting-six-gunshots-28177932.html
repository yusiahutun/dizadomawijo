Armed police shoot knifeman dead in police station car park terrifying B&amp;M shoppers

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, February 9, 2024

Armed officers have shot dead an unknown man wielding a knife police in a police station car park.

A statement released by Derbyshire Police on Friday afternoon says officers spotted a man wandering around their car park off Ascot Drive in Derby at 9.55am today.

The man, whose identity remains unknown to the authorities, was then fired at eight minutes later before any staff or members of public were harmed.

East Midlands Ambulance Service were called to the scene where officers administered first aid until paramedics took him to hospital where he was soon pronounced dead.

Police say officers are working on finding out who the man was and contacting his next of kin.

A cordon is currently in place and Ascot Drive is currently closed and will remain so for some time while enquiries are ongoing.

A number of local businesses that are located within the cordon have taken the decision to close – and shoppers were asked to leave by those stores but police insist nowhere has been evacuated.

As is standard procedure in such situations Derbyshire Constabulary’s Professional Standard’s Department has informed the Independent Office for Police Conduct (IOPC).

Tom Parker, 26, works on Ascot Drive, told DerbyshireLive: "We were in the factory just further down from Ascot Drive and we heard around six gunshots and then came running outside and saw about six to eight police cars came firing past."

Emma Smalley, 28, had been shopping in B&M for about five minutes when the incident occurred.

She told DerbyshireLive: "We heard about six massive bangs and didn’t know what it was at the time. Everyone got evacuated. I’ve had to leave everything in there.

"I just thought it was banging. I didn’t know whether it was a delivery for B&M or someone in the warehouse. It was noisy.

"It’s quite scary to think that if I’d been any later getting there I could’ve witnessed it. I’ve got a three-year-old with me as well."
