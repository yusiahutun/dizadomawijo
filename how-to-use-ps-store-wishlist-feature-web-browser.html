How to Use the Wishlist Feature on the PlayStation Store's Web Browser

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, September 6, 2024

Let's say you're browsing the PlayStation Store and you come across an exciting game you want to make a note of or check back on when a sale drops.

Now, you can easily keep track of all your games-to-buy by adding them to your wishlistâ€”a feature that Sony bizarrely removed and then re-added.

Here's how you can keep track of the games you want to play, and snap them up once they're on sale.

How to Add Games to Your PlayStation Store Wishlist

Adding a game to your PlayStation Store wishlist is simple:

  • Make sure that you're logged into your PS Account on your web browser.
  • Head to the game's page on the PlayStation Store.
  • Click on the heart icon next to Add to Cart.

    • You should then receive a message saying "Added to Wishlist".

    How to View Your Wishlist on the PlayStation Store

    It's great to check your wishlist from time to time, especially to see if any of the games are on sale.

    To view your wishlist, click the heart icon in the top-right of your screen beside your PS Avatar.

    You can also head to your wishlist via a game's page. Just click on the word wishlist when you add and remove games using the heart icon.

    You can also view your wishlist via the PlayStation App on your phone, which will sync up with the web version. Just head to the PS Store section and tap the heart icon.

    If you want to check if any of the games on your wishlist are on sale with your phone, you can control your notifications on the PlayStation mobile app to ensure you get reminders of what's on your wishlist.

    How to Remove Games From Your PlayStation Store Wishlist

    To remove games on your wishlist, deselect the same heart icon on the game's page you used to add that game. You should get a message saying "Removed from your Wishlist".

    If you're scrolling your wishlist and want to remove a game without heading to its page, click the three dots on the far right of the game's purchase screen and then click Remove.

    Keep Some of Sony's Best Games on Your Wishlist

    Adding games to your wishlist is a great way to keep track of what games you want to get next, as well as what games to check when one of Sony's generous sales drops.

    There's always that one game you might keep meaning to play but it slips your mind. Maybe it's one of Sony's outstanding exclusives. With that game on your wishlist, you can keep track of everything all in one place.

    Now, get adding some games!
