Mob Wives stars Marissa Jade and Carla Facciolo show their bikini bodies on Miami beach

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, September 25, 2024

They may be in town for promotional appearances.

But that certainly didn't stop the cast of VH1's Mob Wives from unwinding in Miami, Florida on Saturday.

Marissa Jade, and Carla Facciolo joined their fellow Mob Wives stars Renee Graziano, 47, her sister Big Ang (Angela Raiola), 55, Karen Gravano, 43, to have some fun on the beach.

Bikini babes: Marissa Jade and Carla Facciolo showed off their toned figures at the beach in Miami, Florida on Saturday

Marissa and Carla were especially keen on showing off their fabulous figures.

The twosome put their sculpted physiques on display while frolicking around in tiny two-pieces.

Carla opted for a bright pink and blue swimsuit, as Marissa went with all-black.

Cold? The reality TV stars appeared a bit frigid as they stepped in the ocean

Beach party: The two were joined by their Mob Wives cast mates (L-R) Renee Graziano, Big Ang, and Karen Gravano

Cheers! The cast was seen sipping on cocktails at Fountainbleau bar

As the others preferred to sunbathe, Marissa and Carla cooled off by stepping into the salt water.

The cast was later pictured with cocktails at Fountainbleau bar during P Diddy's RMC weekend.

They were said to have been filming on location for the upcoming season of Mob Wives. 

Though Renee was seen on Saturday, RadarOnline had reported that Renee parted ways with the cast in August.

'I do love VH1 very much, and I support them 100 percent,' she told the news site.

Vibrant:  Carla sported a bold pink and blue two-piece

All about the little things: Carla's bikini was also decorated with petite polka dotted bows 

'I support my sister [Jennifer Graziano — Creator and Executive Producer of Mob Wives], but right now is a chance for Renee.'

An insider explained that the star exited as they were filing season 6, which doesn't have a set release date yet.

'She’s been struggling with Season 6 from the beginning,' the source added.  

Pop of colour: Marissa stood out with a magenta slick on her lips

Chilling: The cast had no problem taking a load off while working on their tans

'After giving six years on the show, she has made a decision to step away from the camera temporarily and to walk away from the show right now.'

Mob Wives has been on air since 2011 and follows the lives of women who are allegedly struggling after their husbands or fathers are committed for Mob-related activities.
