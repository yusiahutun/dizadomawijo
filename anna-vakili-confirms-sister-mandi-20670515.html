Anna Vakili says sister won't go on Love Island 'because she'd get kicked off'

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, July 2, 2024

Anna Vakili has gained a huge Instagram following, brand deals and her own shoe and eyelash range since leaving the Love Island villa this year.

But it's not just the 29-year-old whose life was changed by the ITV reality show, as her sister Mandi earned her own fame while controlling Anna's social media accounts while she was in the villa.

On numerous occasions, Mandi, 27, went viral for her "hilarious" take down of Anna's ex Jordan Hames who broke her heart on the show.

Fans have been begging the beauty to follow her sister's footsteps and, during an exclusive chat with Daily Star Online, Anna has revealed whether she will.

During an event for the launch of her new In The Style collection, she told us: "She's not going to go on Love Island. She doesn't need to."

The pair were reportedly meant to go into series five together but Mandi is said to have been cut by producers at the last minute.

However, Mandi has gone on to achieve her own fame through her Instagram account which now boasts 226,000 followers and she even launched a joint shoe collection with Anna for Simmi London.

Sadly for the pair it's not been all fun and games as they're taunted by trolls daily, but it's no skin off Mandi's nose, according to Anna.
