The 54 Best Sephora Cyber Monday 2023 Deals to Shop Now

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, May 21, 2024

Even if you're not a card carrying Beauty Insider, you can still take advantage of the best Sephora Cyber Monday beauty deals. Today's your last chance to snag up to 50 percent off on culty routine favorites, including markdowns on gift-worthy fragrances, like Mugler's Alien Goddess Eau de Parfum, along with $100 off on special and limited-editions of Dyson's Airwrap Multi-Styler Complete Long.

Plus, Sephora shoppers can continue to enjoy 30 percent off on select brands each day through the end of the sale (we've previously seen price drops on Fresh, Lancôme, and Fenty Beauty by Rihanna, with today-only markdowns on Youth to the People and GXVE by Gwen Stefani).

With so many options to choose from to save on top skin, hair, makeup, and body care essentials, it's practically a no-brainer to replace your seasonal product empties and cross off your holiday shopping list. Read on for a few handpicked deals our editors are eyeing, and keep checking through Monday for our ongoing list of the best Sephora Cyber Monday 2023 deals.

Shop More of the Best Sephora Cyber Monday 2023 Beauty Deals

The best Sephora Cyber Monday 2023 skincare deals

Take your pick of buzzy skincare sets and best-sellers from beloved brands like Kiehl's Since 1851, Guerlain, NuFace, and more on rare sale for a limited-time. With deals this good, your thoughtful holiday skincare gifts can practically wrap themselves—just add a gift bag or wrapping paper.

The best Sephora Cyber Monday 2023 fragrance deals

Round out your collection—or surprise a friend or family member with a top fragrance gift—with 40 percent off on Mugler's celebrity-approved Alien Goddess eau de parfum while it's on deep discount. Bazaar editors are also eyeing major deals on coveted full- and travel-size scents from popular brands like Nette and Viktor&Rolf this weekend.

The best Sephora Cyber Monday 2023 hair deals

Bring the salon home with rare discounts on Sephora's collection of professional-level tools, stylers, and maintenance essentials. Now's the best time to snap up normally pricey items for less, including Drybar's volumizing and top-rated Double Shot Blow-Dryer Brush, plus, Dyson's assortment of salon-ready tools in exclusive colors, like the Supersonic Hair Dryer and Airwrap Multi-Styler Complete.

And you definitely can't forget to snap up daily rotation staples for less while you're shopping: right now, customers can save 30 percent on Olaplex's range of viral hair care and styling products (including the value size version of their buzzy No. 3 Hair Repair Perfector).

The best Sephora Cyber Monday 2023 makeup deals

From price drops on versatile palettes, like Too Faced's 18-shade Better than Chocolate eyeshadow version, to a 40 percent discount on Armani Beauty's luxe Lip Maestro Liquid Matte Lipstick, there are plenty of options worth shopping to elevate your go-to glam essentials. We even spotted a limited-edition brow kit from Anastasia Beverly Hills on markdown that makes an excellent holiday stocking stuffer.

The best Sephora Cyber Monday 2023 body care deals

Sephora's Black Friday 2023 deals make easing into a comprehensive self-care routine a no-brainer. With sales on soothing (and supremely hydrating) body care favorites from brands like Ellis Brooklyn and Olehenriksen up for grabs, we're betting every day will become an at-home spa day for at least the rest of the season.
