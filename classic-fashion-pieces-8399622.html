14 Classic Pieces That Are Wardrobe Must-Haves

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, March 5, 2024

Here's the scoop on a great wardrobe: it's all about high-quality basics. We're talking elevated classics that can be worn season after season without fail. Enter the buzzword of the moment: capsule wardrobe. Now, if you're scratching your head thinking, "huh?" Let us break it down: A capsule wardrobe is packed with those evergreen essentials that are endlessly versatile and will never go out of style. LBDs, snuggly luxe knits, and smart trousers have hung in women’s closets for ages—no seriously, ask your grandma.

Ready for more? From timeless trench coats to perfect pumps, ahead we break down 14 items that are wardrobe must-haves.

Trench Coats

A great trench coat will never go out of style. It's like that one song on repeat—it never gets old. Need something light and breezy for some in-between weather? Check. Gotta handle some unpredictable weather? Sorted. Plus, it’s extremely versatile and easy to style. On those "can't even" days, just throw it on with a tee, some loose jeans, and your go-to sneakers. Or cinch it at the waist for a ladylike look.

Black Blazers

A black blazer is the ultimate layering piece. Style it with jeans, skirts, leather pants—the possibilities are endless.

LBDs

Everyone's gotta have that go-to LBD in their closet—period. Think of it as your wardrobe's MVP, always ready to save the day. Daytime brunch? Just toss on a sleek blazer, some cute socks, and loafers for a smart casual look. Oh, and those surprise cocktail plans? No sweat. Slip into some pumps or strappy sandals, add a dash of bling, and bam—chic and fabulous!

White Button Downs

A crisp white shirt epitomizes effortless chic. When worn alone, it pairs perfectly with jeans, trousers, or skirts. Or if you’re looking to add depth to your look, layer it under blazers or knits to elevate your ensemble.

Diamond Studs

There’s truly nothing more timeless than a pair of diamond stud earrings. Perfect for everyday wear they add a touch of sparkle to any look.

Perfect Pumps

While we're all for a sky-high strappy stiletto in a bold color or an embellished satin mule, some moments call for something a bit more pared back. Cue the classic pump, destined to be a staple in your wardrobe thanks to its versatility and timeless look. Whether paired with trousers for the office or an LBD for a cocktail party, a pump is always a foolproof choice.

Reliable Flats

On those days when heels aren't on the agenda, ballet flats, and loafers step in as the perfect flat alternatives. These chic options are ultra-classic and go with almost everything—from jeans to trousers, dresses to skirts, and everything in between.

Great Denim

Splurging on high-quality denim is well worth it, especially when you realize you only need that one perfect pair. The secret lies in the fit. Once you've discovered “the one,” you’ll never have to buy another pair again.

White T-Shirts

This one is a no-brainer: A white T-shirt is a must-have in your wardrobe. It's a great layering piece for a sleek outfit. It serves as a great base under crew neck sweaters, and blazers. But don't underestimate its standalone charm—it looks just as good with tailored trousers or a sexy skirt.

Rich Knits

There’s nothing like rich knits from cozy cashmere turtlenecks to luxe mohair cardigans. A good sweater is a wintertime necessity.

Touches of Gold

You simply can't go wrong with gold accessories. Be it a statement chain link necklace, a stacked set of bracelets, or a sophisticated watch, a touch of gold will elevate any look.

Sleek Belts

Don't forget about the magic of a sleek belt. Heading out in just a white tee and jeans? throw on a belt to break things up and give the look more dimension. Is the oversized shirtdress a bit too oversized? Don’t fret, a great belt can be used to cinch in your waist and give any outfit that extra flair.

Investment Bags

In the world of capsule wardrobes, it's all about those long-lasting gems. And let's be real, a solid bag is at the top of the list. Just think about the Chanel classic flap—it's iconic, always in style, and you can rock it pretty much every day.

Timeless Trousers

A great pair of timeless trousers never disappoints. Rock them with a tee and sneakers for that effortlessly cool vibe, or amp it up with a blazer for a sharp statement.
