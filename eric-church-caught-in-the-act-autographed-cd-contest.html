Win an Autographed Copy of Eric Churchs Caught in the Act Album

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, February 12, 2024

Fans can experience one of the more electric live shows in country music anytime they want with the release of Eric Church's new 'Caught in the Act' live album. Taste of Country has teamed up with the superstar to give fans a chance to win an autographed copy of the record, which is available at digital retailers and in stores now.

The disc contains 17 songs, including 10 from his most recent ACM Album of the Year Award-winning project, 'Chief.' Radio hits like 'Springsteen,' 'Drink in My Hand' and Homeboy' are paired with live hits like 'Smoke a Little Smoke' and 'Lotta Boot Left to Fill.' 'Pledge Allegiance to the Hag,' 'Jack Daniels' and 'How 'Bout You' are other Church hits found on the live project.

To win, follow the instructions in the entry form below. Start by joining the Taste of Country email list to earn two entry points, and then follow @tasteofcountry or @ericchurch on Twitter for even more chances to win! There are up to seven different ways to enter.

One winner will be chosen to receive the autographed copy of 'Caught in the Act' when the contest ends on May 16, 2013 at 11:59PM ET. Two additional runners-up will win non-autographed copies of 'Caught in the Act' for their listening pleasure.
