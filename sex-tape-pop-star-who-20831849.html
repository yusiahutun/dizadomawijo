Sex tape pop star who quit showbiz desperate to have 'revenge porn' taken down

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 29, 2024

A pop star who quit showbiz after her sex tape went viral is stil desperately trying to get the leaked material taken down.

Rabi Pirzada, 30, announced her retirement after the revenge porn leak last week.

The Pakistani singer has made headlines in the past for her controversial posts, including one of her wearing a suicide bomber's vest.

She also made a number of provocative statements about India's Prime Minister Narendra Modi, particularly over the crisis in the disputed Kashmir region.

On Friday, she became the victim of a humiliating privacy breach when nude pictures and videos of her were shared online.

The hashtag #RabiPirzada was the top trend on Twitter in Pakistan for much of the next few days.

Rabi is reportedly still battling to have the explicit images and video removed from the internet, but she sent her request for removal to the wrong government agency.

After the leak, she announced she was quitting the entertainment industry and filed a petition with Pakistan's Federal Investigation Agency (FIA) to tackle the spread of her sex tape.

But media reports say this is outside of the FIA's powers, and the request was forwarded to the Pakistan Telecommunication Authority (PTA).

Rabi has reportedly handed her phone over to authorities, to tackle the supposed hack.

Speaking to Pakistani publication The Express Tribune, Rabi said: "I am sick and tired of all the fake news surrounding the issue, everyone is selling their own versions of things that never happened.

"The FIA has been very cooperative and supportive, and they have not refused me anything."

She also thanked the public for their support after the leak.

It still isn't known how the material made it online, but one theory is that the "revenge porn" was leaked by an angry or jealous ex-boyfriend.

Others have suggested that the star was targeted for her controversial political statements.

She sparked fury last month for a post comparing Indian PM Modi to Adolf Hitler, after sharing a photograph of herself in a suicide vest.

Along with the picture, she included the hashtag "#ModiHitler".

Earlier, she shared a video of herself posing with large snakes on a crocodile, pledging that the reptiles would "feast" on Modi.
