Solero slushie cocktails become hit on TikTok and they're perfect for summer

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, April 3, 2024

Solero slushie cocktails have become all the rage on TikTok and one user's mouth-watering recipe is going viral.

In the recipe uploaded by user @evanmcintosh_, Solero ice cream is blended with ice, frozen mango, passion fruit juice, vodka and Malibu liqueur.

The indulgent drink is served in a Martini glass and topped with a slice of fresh passionfruit – and it looks an absolute treat.

Unsurprisingly, the video was "liked" more than 64,000 times since it was uploaded last week and hundreds of people left comments, with many saying they were itching to try it out for themselves at the first sign of summer.
