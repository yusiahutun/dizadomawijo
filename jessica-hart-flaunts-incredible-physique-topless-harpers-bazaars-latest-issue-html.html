She's known for her natural look and striking features.

So it's no surprise that model Jessica Hart has graced the cover of Harper's Bazaar Australia's June/July edition, The Beauty Issue.

The gap-toothed blonde's true beauty is on exhibit in a dedicated spread and cover, wearing simple white pieces while her glowing skin and face is the star of the show.

White hot! Jessica Hart is a glowing goddess on the June/July cover of Harper's Bazaar Australia

On the exquisite cover revealed on Monday, Jessica wears a white bustier dress, displaying her flawless decolletage, and her hair is swept up in a beige wrap.
