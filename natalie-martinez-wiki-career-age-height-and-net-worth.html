Career of Natalie Martinez

Natalie Martinez made her television debut in 2006 as Michelle Miller in 55 episodes of the evening serial “Fashion House” on My Network TV. In 2007, she was cast as Pilar Martin, the main character in the telenovela “Saints & Sinners.”

She also made an appearance in the TV comedy “Sons of Tucson” in 2010. She made an appearance as Det. Ariana Sanchez in the police drama “Detroit 1-8-7” that same year. She also had appearances in several television programs, including “CSI: NY” in 2012, “Under the Dome” in 2013, “Matador” in 2014, and “Secrets and Lies” in 2015.

Additionally, she played the part of Alicia Mendez in the cast of “Kingdom” from 2015 to 2017. She then made appearances in “APB” in 2017 and “From Dusk till Dawn: The Series” in 2016.

When it comes to Natalie’s movies, her first one was the 2008 release “Death Race.” After that, she was cast in 2011’s “End of Watch,” “The Baytown Outlaws,” and “Magic City Memoirs.”

She also played Natalie Barrow in “Broken City” in 2013, Madeline in “Self/less” in 2015, and King’s Message in “The Land” in 2016. This actress played Olivia in the horror-thriller movie “Keep Watching” in 2017. Additionally, she made her voice debut in the animated musical comedy “Ugly Dolls” in 2019.
