Brooklyn Beckham reunites with rumoured girlfriend Sonia Ben Ammar in LA

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, May 30, 2024

    Brooklyn Beckham reignited speculation that he is dating French actress Sonia Ben Ammar after the pair were spotted together in Los Angeles at the weekend. The teenagers, who are both 16, were pictured looking close as they left the Hollywood Bowl music venue together on Saturday night.

    The eldest son of David and Victoria Beckham took his love interest to the CBS Radio concert, which featured performances from Rihanna and Sam Smith. Brooklyn looked typically stylish in a black leather jacket, white t-shirt and skinny jeans, while Sonia was dressed down in black skinny jeans and a printed t-shirt.

    Brooklyn Beckham and Sonia Ben Ammar attended a concert together in LA

    The annual concert attracted a plethora of high-profile stars including Kendall Jenner, Gigi Hadid and Joe Jonas. Also in attendance was Chloë Grace Moretz – whom Brooklyn was linked to in 2014.

    Brooklyn and Sonia first sparked dating rumours in September, when they were seen strolling around the streets of Paris. The teen pin up also shared an arty photo of Sonia from behind, taking in a sweeping view of the City of Light.

    The pretty brunette first featured on his Instagram page in August, when she appeared in a group photo alongside Brooklyn. "Fun night with these people," the budding photographer had written alongside it.

    According to , the student and the French actress met earlier this year during a family holiday to the Maldives. Sonia also spent time with the Beckhams in LA over the summer.

    Sonia, whose acting credits include drama and animated film , is thought to live in Europe, while Brooklyn is based in West London, where he goes to school.

    The family once again spent the school holidays together in Los Angeles, with Brooklyn's four-year-old sister Harper pictured enjoying a horse riding lesson with mum Victoria on Thursday.
