Miley Cyruss Sister Talks &quot;Flowers&quot; Theories About Liam Hemsworth

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, April 10, 2024

Miley Cyrus's "Flowers," seemingly about her breakup from ex-husband Liam Hemsworth, topped the music charts in Hemsworth’s home country of Australia, broke Spotify's one-week streaming record, and sparked a plethora of fan theories about the music video containing particularly shady Easter eggs about Hemsworth.

One fan theory is that Cyrus's gold dress alludes to Hemsworth and Hunger Games actress Jennifer Lawrence having a fling. Another is that the track is Cyrus's response to Bruno Mars's song "When I Was Your Man," which Hemsworth allegedly dedicated to her while they were together. None of this has been confirmed by Cyrus—they're all rumors—but her sister Brandi Cyrus spoke with Wells Adams on the Your Favorite Thing podcast about the fan theories directly.

Corey Nickols//Getty Images

Liam Hemsworth and Miley Cyrus; Brandi Cyrus.

She didn't confirm or deny anything outright. Instead, she just marveled over her little sister's genius.

"Miley's new song, all the tea … and all the narratives that fans are making on TikTok. It's so good," Brandi said, per Us Weekly. "The song did come out on his birthday—was that on purpose? I don't know. Can't say. Genius, though."

The narratives fans have created "makes Miley seem like an absolute genius," Brandi continued. "And it's just too good. Like, every day I wake up to a new one. The first one was something about this Bruno Mars song. They're like, 'Liam played the Bruno Mars song at their wedding! And this is a response to the Bruno Mars song.' That one's funny."

She added, "Then there was, 'The suit she's wearing is the suit Liam wore at the Avengers premiere, where he told her that she needed to behave for once. And so this is a f*ck you because she's in the suit.' Hilarious.

"Then the other one was, 'The house is the house where Liam used to take people [to] cheat on her with.' The narratives are f*cking hilarious, but it's so great. The best PR that could ever happen because it gets people talking about the song on TikTok and it's everywhere. And it's just so fun to watch it all play out. Miley fans, I love ya."

When Wells asked Brandi if any of the theories were true, Brandi played coy. "I'm not here to say that's true and what's not true," she said. "Only Miley knows the truth."
