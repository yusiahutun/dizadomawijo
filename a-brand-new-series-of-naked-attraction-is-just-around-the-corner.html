A brand new series of NAKED ATTRACTION is just around the corner! Fri 18 January 2019 13:15

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, April 27, 2024

Naked Attraction may be one of the most bizarre TV series in history.

But that doesn’t mean we won’t stop watching it! 

The dating show that dangles genitals on display and has contestants stripping completely naked is set to return for a fresh run in 2019.

Here is everything we know about Naked Attraction 2019!

  • Naked Attraction: Who is presenter Anna Richardson?
  • Would you bare it all? How to apply for Naked Attraction
  • Viewers in SHOCK as Ibiza Weekender star bares all coming out of the shower

Naked Attraction 2019

The brand new series of Naked Attraction launches on Tuesday, January 22nd.

Series 4 will continue every Tuesday night on Channel 4, at 10:35 pm.

Most series air for around five or six episodes, although there are always repeats muddled within the weekly Freeview TV schedule.

Watch Naked Attraction Online

You can catch up with any missed Naked Attraction episodes online via the official Channel 4 player, ALL4.

Each episode should be uploaded shortly after it finishes airing on Tuesday evenings.

ALL4 is also host to all of the previous four Naked Attraction series, with over 20 episodes available to binge-watch.

Naked Attraction is also available on Sky Go but not streaming platforms such as Netflix, Hayu and Now TV.

Anna Richardson.

Who is Naked Attraction Presenter Anna Richardson?

Anna Richardson is a 48-year-old TV presenter and journalist.

She has a lot of experience breaching tender subjects having presented documentary series such as The Sex Education Show, Supersize vs Superskinny, Supershoppers and You Are What You Eat.

She joined the Naked Attraction when it first launched on Channel 4 in 2016.

Anna is not married. However, she has been in a long-term relationship with fellow media personality Sue Perkins for four years.
