20 prayers to show appreciation to someone who helped you

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, May 21, 2024

Prayers are powerful. Through prayers, believers talk to their God and get what they ask. Many people across the world pray for various reasons. Some pray to appreciate life, others ask for strength or present their complaints to God, others offer prayers to show appreciation to someone, and others want to communicate with their creator.

Every prayer counts, and no one should judge the other for either having a short or long prayer. Christians are encouraged to pray wherever they are. You can pray when you are at home, at work, at school, and even when you are walking.

As a Christian, do not forget to offer prayer messages for your helper, family, and anyone else in your life. Express gratitude in prayer as much as you make requests in prayer. God loves a grateful Christian, and you are encouraged to always thank the Lord for what you have before asking for more.

Appreciation prayer to a friend

How do you pray to appreciate a friend? There is no formula in prayer as you kneel and start talking to God. Mention the good things about your friends, and thank God for letting them be a part of your life. Here are prayers for appreciating friends:

Jehovah, Father, I come before you this morning, grateful for the gift of life and health. I am grateful for all you have provided for me. I am happy for the good life, and the gift of friends. Dear God, you have given me friends who have become siblings. My father and Lord, I could never take the friends you have given me for granted. I pray that you bless each friend I have and make them live fulfilling lives. Amen.
