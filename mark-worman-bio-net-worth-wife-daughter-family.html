Mark Worman’s Bio, Wiki

Mark Worman was born on 21st August 1962, in Springfield, Oregon, USA. He was born under the birth sign Leo. Since his childhood, he was interested in bikes and gradually shifted to cars.

Worman struggled a lot while growing up. As he lost his father when he was just 12-year-old. He was raised under the shadow of his mother. Worman himself was battling with the health problems and his mother had difficulty raising the family on his own.

Regarding his educational qualification, he attended Springfield Jr. High School, but dropped out halfway through 9th grade. However, he completed his high school diploma through a program at Lane Community College.

At the age of 16, Worman got his first car 1970’s 383 two-barrel Dodge Charger with a burnt orange color. And finally, in 1985, he started his three bay-auto shops Welby’s Car Care Center. Prior to that. Worman worked in some local garage for some years. Later on, he was able to show his skill to the world of entertainment through the TV show Graveyard Carz.

After that, Worman was appointed as a manager of The Division Production, and as of now, he is the CEO of the same company. Besides this, Mark is also known for some of the television shows which also includes Trinket (2014) and LA Business Today (2008).
