12 Best Pink Lipsticks - New Pink Lip Products for 2022

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Every product on this page was chosen by a Harper's BAZAAR editor. We may earn commission on some of the items you choose to buy.

1

NARS Schiap

1

NARS Schiap

Did you know? It's called Schiap after Elsa Schiaparelli, the designer who made the shade Shocking Pink her signature. 

2

Charlotte Tilbury Matte Revolution Lipstick Pillow Talk

2

Charlotte Tilbury Matte Revolution Lipstick Pillow Talk

This your-lips-but-better iteration on a neutral rose is so popular, that Charlotte Tilbury has since expanded it into an entire collection of products.

3

FENTY BEAUTY by Rihanna Stunna Lip Paint Longwear Fluid Lip Color Unlocked

3

FENTY BEAUTY by Rihanna Stunna Lip Paint Longwear Fluid Lip Color Unlocked

Rihanna's latest Stunna Lip Paint is the shade Unlocked, an electric fuchsia that looks equally bold and fierce on all skin tones.

4

Yves Saint Laurent Rouge Pur Couture The Slim Matte Lipstick Contrary Fuchsia

4

Yves Saint Laurent Rouge Pur Couture The Slim Matte Lipstick Contrary Fuchsia

The geometric bullet on YSL's The Slim matte lipsticks makes quick work of tracing sharp lines around your Cupid's bow. That surgical precision is key when the shade is as bright as Contrary Fuchsia.

5

MAC Shadescent Lipstick Candy Yum-Yum

5

MAC Shadescent Lipstick Candy Yum-Yum

One of MAC's most-iconic shades behind Ruby Woo and Lady Danger, this electrified bubblegum-pink is not for the faint of heart.

6

Revlon Super Lustrous Lipstick Cherries in the Snow

6

Revlon Super Lustrous Lipstick Cherries in the Snow

Now 29% Off

Some might say that Cherries In the Snow, the iconic lipstick shade dating back to the 1950s, is red. It's not, but it's close enough that you can consider it the pink lipstick for those who only wear red.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

7

TOM FORD Lip Color Flamingo

7

TOM FORD Lip Color Flamingo

This creamy and ultra-pigmented lipstick formula is as pink as the shade name suggests.

8

Estee Lauder Pure Color Envy Sculpting Lipstick Tumultuous Pink

8

Estee Lauder Pure Color Envy Sculpting Lipstick Tumultuous Pink

This cool-toned berry is one of the most comfortable shades to wear on this list, thanks to the ultra-moisturizing formula.

9

Giorgio Armani Beauty Lip Maestro 504

9

Giorgio Armani Beauty Lip Maestro 504

If you're looking for a bold pink lipstick that will last all day (and maybe into the next), try this. The Lip Maestro formula is famous for feeling nearly weightless, leaving a semi-matte finish, and keeping lips from drying out.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

10

PAT McGRATH LABS MatteTrance Lipstick Full Panic

10

PAT McGRATH LABS MatteTrance Lipstick Full Panic

This fuchsia formula from Pat McGrath is so good, it's converted even matte-lipstick-haters into fans.

11

Kat Von D Everlasting Liquid Lipstick Lolita

11

Kat Von D Everlasting Liquid Lipstick Lolita

Since its release in 2015, Lolita has become Kat Von D's highest-rated shade, with one tube sold every minute. The dusty rose even inspired a collection of eyeshadows and liner.

12

LAURA MERCIER Velour Extreme Matte Lipstick It Girl

12

LAURA MERCIER Velour Extreme Matte Lipstick It Girl

Now 15% Off

Fans of ultra-matte lipstick are crazy for this formula, which leaves behind the brightest pigment we've even seen with an extreme matte finish. Somehow—through magic— it doesn't feel like it sucks the life out of your lips.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

13

KYLIE COSMETICS Lip Kit Posie K

13

KYLIE COSMETICS Lip Kit Posie K

One of Kylie Jenner's earliest shades, Posie K is a cool rose that looks soft and pretty on lips of all ages and skin tones. The Lip Kit matte formula can be a little drying, but the formula lasts through anything and everything.

14

Dior Lip Glow

14

Dior Lip Glow

Calling this a lipstick is a little but of a misnomer. This universal lip-plumping and hydrating balm reacts to your own unique skin chemistry to leave behind the perfect sheer was of pink color. You're going to want to toss one in every bag you own.

15

L'Oréal Paris Infallible Pro-Last Lip Color Passionate Petal

15

L'Oréal Paris Infallible Pro-Last Lip Color Passionate Petal

L'Oréal Pari's dual-ended Infallible lip colors are meant to last through three-plus meals and maybe a day or two, until you decide to remove it with an oil-based cleanser. The clear balm on the other end keeps lips from drying out.
