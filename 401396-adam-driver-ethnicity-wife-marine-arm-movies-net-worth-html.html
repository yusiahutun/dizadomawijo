Adam enlisted to join the United States Marine Corps after the September 11 attacks. He was assigned an 81mm mortarman to the 1st Battalion, 1st Marines Weapons Company.

Acting

Adam Driver started his career in the film industry after graduating from Juilliard. He appeared in off-Broadway and Broadway productions in New York City. He occasionally worked as a waiter and busboy before landing his acting roles.

His debut TV role was as a repentant witness and reluctant accomplice to an unsolved assault in the final episode of The Unusuals. He rose to fame after starring in the comedy-drama series titled Girls. He played the role of Adam Sackler, the unstable boyfriend of Hannah Horvath.
