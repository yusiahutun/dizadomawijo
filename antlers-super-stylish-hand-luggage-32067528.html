Antler's super stylish hand luggage case for easyJet, TUI and British Airways now has 65 off

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, May 31, 2024

We've found the 'perfect size' designer cabin bag for the likes of easyJet, TUI and British Airways and it's now less than £100. If you're looking for a stylish suitcase for your 2024 travels, this deal from Antler might have come at a good time.

The Tiber Cabin Case has a sleek design that any fashion-conscious traveller would love. Not only does it look cool, but it's also practical and has been reduced to just £99, making high-end luggage more affordable.

This cabin case by Antler is now a real bargain, with over £65 off, The Mirror reports. One reviewer described it as the "perfect size for a couple of days away."

The Tiber has a slim shape with rounded corners that are compact enough for use on most UK airlines.

It measures a neat 55 x 40 x 20 cm and weighs just 2.5 kg (5.5 lbs), so it can be used on many popular carriers including British Airways, TUI, Jet2, easyJet, Virgin Atlantic and more.

Despite being lightweight and easy to handle, the Antler Tiber Cabin Case is sturdy enough for frequent air travel. It features a Makrolon polycarbonate shell designed to withstand the rigours of flying. The trendy charcoal colour is subtle yet stylish, and the scalloped surface helps keep dirt and scratches at bay.

Inside, this spacious suitcase can hold up to 36 litres of your holiday essentials. It also has a fully zippered compartment to keep your clothes wrinkle-free and luggage straps to keep everything tidy.

The £99 Antler Tiber comes with a fixed TSA combination lock to keep your belongings safe. Plus, it has a 10-year warranty, so you'll be using this suitcase for many trips to come.

Antler is not just known for its stylish designs and roomy interiors, but also for its well-engineered features.

This model has Hinomoto 360-degree wheels that glide silently beside you. The retractable trolley handle makes the case easy to manoeuvre, responding to every turn you make.

If the Tiber is not quite right for your needs, Antler currently has more great deals on some of their best-loved collections including 40% off Prestwick soft side cabin suitcases and up to 50% off selected Clifton cases.

With the holiday season fast approaching other luggage retailers including Amazon and Luggage Superstore also have deals on suitcases and cabin bags that you can buy today.
