Dierks Bentley Announces His Gold &amp; Gravel Tour

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, March 7, 2024

NASHVILLE, TN (CelebrityAccess) — Country singer-songwriter Dierks Bentley announced plans for a major summer tour with more than 30 dates scheduled across North America from June through September 2024.

Produced by Live Nation, Bentley’s “Gravel & Gold” tour is scheduled to get underway on June 6th at Dickies Arena in Forth Worth and wrap at the American Family Insurance Amphitheater in Milwaukee on September 21st.

Along the way, Bentley is slated to perform at a diverse array of venues, from The Saratoga Performing Arts Center and the Mohegan Sun Arena to Red Rocks and the Cajundome.

Both Chase Rice and Lee Brice have signed on as direct support for the tour, and the entourage will be joined by a lineup of special guests that includes Graham Barham, Tyler Braden, Kaitlin Butts, Mae Estes, Ella Langley, Randy Rogers Band, Zach Top, Tanner Usrey, Bella White and more.

“The Gravel & Gold tour has been the best of my career, and I’m not ready to leave it behind,” explains Bentley. “We are so locked in with this production that it allows me to be living right in the moment with the fans, and that’s the magic! I’m bringing along some old friends and some newer artists who are all putting their own unique spin on country, bluegrass, and Americana music. I can’t wait to watch their shows and hopefully sing with them too. Those collaborations are a highlight of my summer every year.”
