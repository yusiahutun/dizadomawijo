Jeff Fisher has an estimated net worth of $20 million. Previously hired as the head coach of Tennessee Titans team, Jeff Fisher eventually served as the head coach of St. Louis Rams. Before he proceeded with his coaching career, Jeff Fisher started as a football player under the USC team. He was actually drafted by the Chicago Bears in the 7th round of 1981 NFL Draft.

When his career ended, Jeff Fisher still wanted to become actively involved in professional football. He became a football coordinator and when Pardi was fired, Fisher assumed his position as the head coach of the Houston Oilers/Tennessee Titans for the remaining 6 games of the season. The team retained Fisher as their coach even after the season ended. Jeff Fisher eventually became the head coach of St. Louis Rams.
