Celebrities Love Wearing Bras With Jackets and Cardigans, Creating 2020's Bracket Fashion Trend

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, April 5, 2024

If the mere thought of putting on a bra makes you shudder — especially after months at home, social distancing in comfy clothes — we definitely feel you. Despite the fact that our drawers are filled with classic, reliable options, we don't exactly miss fussing with those straps and annoying underwire. Celebrities, on the other hand? They seem to be all about it, continuing to style the wardrobe staple underneath jackets and cardigans, even as we head into cooler weather.

Seeing so many brackets (bras + jackets) day after day, we're almost (keyword, almost) convinced to give the trend a try ourselves. Whether the look consists of a matching set, color-coordinated options, or more of a crop top than an actual bralette, there are plenty of ways to pull it off with the clothes that are already in our closet. Considering we're still spending a lot of time indoors, now is the perfect time to experiment.

You Can Wear a Full Set

Hailey Bieber took a note from Katie Holmes' viral bra and cardigan combo, pairing her own knit set with jeans and kitten heels.

Or Style Your Bra Under an Oversized Jacket

Despite the fact that Alicia Keys wore this red jacket and black bra while performing, the look would easily transition into real life, too, possibly for a fun date night.

Go With a Crop Top

Want a way to spruce up your leggings? Take a cue from Irina Shayk and add a matching crop top. Then, grab your favorite basic button-down, colorful sneakers, and your outfit is complete.

Or Just Double Up

Suki Waterhouse traded a traditional bra for a super cropped cardigan instead, layering it under a thin puffer and adding high-waisted, baggy jeans.

Pair It With Other Seasonal Trends

Unsurprisingly, Bella Hadid gave her bracket a '90s twist. A short-sleeved button-down took the place of a top layer, and she once again convinced us to invest in khakis.

Play With Around With Proportions

You don't need to show a ton of stomach when following this trend. For example, model Josephine Skriver let her midsection peek out just a tiny bit when wearing a cutout crop top with pink high-waisted pants. She then made an even bolder statement with the help of a longer, duster-like jacket.

Color Coordinate

Model Hana Cross' cardigan and bra aren't a perfect match, but they're still in the same color family, making the look just as cute.

Think Fancy

Lily Rose Depp gave the bracket a fancy twist with the help of a structured jacket, printed bra, and metallic heels while attending the Chanel Spring '21 show.

Prep For the Future

It makes sense that actresses and models are still into brackets. The combination continues to pop up on the runway, including Alberta Ferretti's Spring '21 show, so clearly the look has staying power and will be around for a bit longer.
