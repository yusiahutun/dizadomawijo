The Swiss International The Vistana Lekki , Lekki, Nigeria

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, April 20, 2024

The Swiss International The Vistana – Lekki is a world class hotel and the flagship of the Swiss International Hotels & Resorts in West Africa. The hotel is strategically and ideally located at Victoria Garden City in the heart of Lekki and a direct drive to the Victoria Island, Dangote Refinery and 55 minutes’ drive to and from the international airport.

 A   4-Star   hotel with “Out of the World” Service experience, The Swiss International The Vistana – Lekki features over 50 stylish and well-appointed Superior Rooms and Suites including Presidential Suites with state of the art furnishings. With an impeccable themed restaurant with ‘finest multinational cuisines menu’, over four function rooms/conference, a provision for super health club and physique academy, outdoor swimming pool caters for both Leisure and business travelers.

Swiss International The Vistana is located in a prime location in Victoria Garden City, Lekki, Nigeria. Known as the new business sprout of Lagos, Lekki house lots of business ranging from Oil & Gas, FMCG, Real Estate, Leisure hub and it’s today the center for Nigeria’s leisure industry. Located in the South-Western Nigeria, this is a serene and secure environment.

 Swiss International The Vistana is approximately 55 minutes to the Muritala Mohammed International Airport and 15 minutes from the new Dangote Refinery.

Rooms
Superior Rooms are Contemporary rooms, 25 sqm with a wall mounted smart TV and in room bathroom.
Deluxe Rooms are Contemporary rooms, 30 sqm with a wall mounted smart TV and in room bathroom.
Swiss Select Rooms are Luxurious en-suite, 100 sqm with settee living and working area, spacious bathroom with deluxe bathroom amenities.
Executive Suites are Luxurious Suite, 100 sqm with separate living and working area, spacious bathroom with deluxe bathroom amenities.
Presidential Suites are Luxurious Suite, 296 sqm with 3 bedrooms and a separate living and working area. A spacious en suite bathroom with deluxe bathroom amenities.
Added facilities are Mini mart and boutique, Hairdressing saloon and barbers shop. Spa treatment on request.
SWISS FLAVOUR RESTAURANT
Vibrant & Elegant. The Swiss Flavour’s Menu focuses on fresh and healthy dishes with portion sizes to fit your appetite. A wide selection of wine from around the world is also available.
TED & CO BAR
Unwind after a long day sipping aperitifs with friends and colleagues in our Ted & Co Bar.
INSPIRATIONS POOL BAR
Sit back and relax in the comfortable open space at the swimming pool, while sipping signature local and signature cocktails. A perfect place to mingle with other hotel guests, the locals and business travelers.
CONFERENCING FACILITY
Swiss International The Vistana-Lekki offers comprehensive meeting space where each room varies in size and layout. Whether for business meetings, seminars, conferences or private functions, our dedicated team will anticipate all your needs to facilitate all your Meetings and Events. The meeting rooms can accommodate up to 450 people and are located on the ground and 1st floor in a quiet atmosphere. The large conference hall, fully air conditioned, possesses its own powerful LED Screen, Control Room, Light mixer system with a 6-channel mixing console, large speakers, and selection of wireless microphones, LED projector and state of the art up –to date ICT facilities.
GYM
To keep up with your regular exercise, our gym is equipped with the latest equipment for you to keep fit during your stay.
SWIMMING POOL
Enjoy a dip in the swimming pool or sit back and relax while sipping signature cocktails at the pool bar.
FAMILIES
Swiss International The Vistana-Lekki, welcomes families and provides connecting rooms, extra beds and baby cots on request. Trips, excursions can be organized for a family day outing.
We assure you the best-personalized service from our dedicated employees.
ROOM SERVICE
Guests can also enjoy the delicately prepared Room Service in the privacy comfort of their rooms.
