About

Johnny Simmons is a 37 year old American Actor. Born Johnny James Simmons on 28th November, 1986 in Montgomery, Alabama, he is famous for Evan Almighty in a career that spans 2004–present (on hiatus). His zodiac sign is Sagittarius.

Emma Watson is a 33 year old British Actress. Born Emma Charlotte Duerre Watson on 15th April, 1990 in Paris, France and educated at Headington School, Oxford, England, UK and Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts in London, England, UK, she is famous for her roles as Hermione Granger in the Harry Potter film series, Samantha “Sam” Button in the 2012 coming-of-age drama film The Perks of Being a Wallflower, Belle in the 2017 musical romantic fantasy film Beauty and the Beast, Margaret “Meg” March in the 2019 coming-of-age period drama film Little Women, Pauline Fossil in the 2007 BBC made-for-television dance drama film Ballet Shoes, Lucy Armstrong in the 2011 biographical drama film My Week with Marilyn, Lena in the 2015 historical thriller film Colonia, Ila in the 2014 epic biblical drama film Noah, and Reverend Iris in the 2015 Comic Relief special episode of the BBC1 sitcom The Vicar of Dibley titled The Bishop of Dibley. Her zodiac sign is Aries.
