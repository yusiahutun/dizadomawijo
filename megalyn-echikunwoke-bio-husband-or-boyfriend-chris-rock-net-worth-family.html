Megalyn Echikunwoke: Bio

The pretty actress is the descendant of a Nigerian tribal leader, so she has African royal blood. Her father, Onigwe Versato Echikunwake, is a Nigerian Igbo man from a royal family. Her mother, Anita Laurie, is a German-Scottish-Irish woman. She was born on May 28, 1983, in Spokane, Washington, but grew up on the Navajo Indian reservation in Chinle, Arizona with her two siblings, Mike (brother) and Misty (sister) Echiunwake. The last name of the Echikunwake family stands for “leader of the men”.

Encounter With Fame

The 1.69-meter tall actress developed into a cool, reserved woman whose beauty blends with her brilliance. With an attractive, slender body, a perfectly curved face, a soothing smile, and a sweet, melodic voice that matches her acting talent, she was discovered in 1997 at the age of only 14 in a summer art camp. She spent the rest of the year auditioning for roles after spending some time at the camp. The actress could hardly believe her luck the following year when she got the chance to appear on the Steve Harvey Show before her role in a television mini-series called Creature.

She gradually gained popularity after that, not only for her roles in numerous major groundbreaking films, shows, and television series such as CSI: Miami as Dr. Tara Price, Late Night, Law and Order, Supernatural, American Dad, Ghosted, Night School but also for her unique ability to move between comedy and drama.

She demonstrated this particularity in a television series called “The 4400”, in which she was the adult mysterious Isabelle. Unfortunately, she left the series in 2007 when it was canceled. Megalyn’s other works include an appearance in Spyder Games as a singer and in the first season of 24 years as the daughter of David Palmer. She also appeared in Buffy the Vampire Slayer (2003) and sang the title character in the animation Vixen and played her in Arrows. She was the first black superhero in the DC Comic universe to play a superhero with the magical ability to summon animal powers. Megalyn Echikunwoke revealed her gratitude for this role by giving her the opportunity to show supernatural powers and hopes to show more of this in her films.

Although everything she has achieved in her career has basically come from acting, Megalyn also has a preference for singing. Her secret desire to become a singer was summed up in a film called Who do you love, in which she played a singer who fought against heroin addiction. She has more than forty film and television credits and seems ready to get more roles.
