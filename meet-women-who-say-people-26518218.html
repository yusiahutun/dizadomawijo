Meet the women who say people are jealous of their big boobs

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, April 6, 2024

Having big boobs can be a blessing and a curse – with these women saying they're often judged over their looks.

The curvy ladies are often trolled because of their figures and believe it's because people are jealous of them.

Here, Daily Star has taken a look at the women who have spoken about the struggles of having bigger breasts.

Mikaela Testa

OnlyFans star Mikaela Testa hit back at trolls who accused her of being "fake" and said she she is tired of "jealous" women making comments about her body.

The 23-year-old woman from Queensland, Australia, wasn't impressed when trolls accused her boobs of being "fake" after she posted a photo of herself in a bikini.

Her photo was captioned with the word "melons" and garnered more than 25,000 likes in a matter of days, with trolls accusing Mikaela of being "plastic" and "gross".

Mikaela highlighted the negativity she received on Instagram. She responded: "Every hate comment I get is actually from women and it's only ever about my body and nothing else.

"They have problems with themselves. Remember there are girls like this everywhere, it's called jealousy and projection. A beautiful woman who is happy with herself would never act like this."

Emily Hawthorne

Emily Hawthorne, 22, innocently shared a photo of herself in a low-cut dress to promote a sushi restaurant for Valentine's Day. She had no idea that soon vicious trolls would bully her for her breasts, resulting in her having a panic attack.

Emily said: "I happened to check the comments to see how the photo was doing and the number of vile things being said about me was truly shocking.

"I was physically shaking. I had a panic attack and I thought: 'I want to curl up into a ball and delete myself.'

"What was genuinely dreadful was that most of the comments were from women, saying rude things and tagging their friends. I was always taught that women should support women, so to see the harsh comments from females was horrible."

Shirley Flynn

A woman with 38J boobs says that while many love her curves, she has been targeted by trolls in recent months.

Shirley Flynn says some keyboard warriors are "jealous" of her enviable figure and in an exclusive chat with Daily Star, she revealed the lewd and sometimes mean comments she receives.

Shirley explained: “I show my boobs off because that's what the men want and it's part of my online character ‘Busty_malone’ it's her image and her trademark is in her name.

“I find it quite amusing that some people would be so stupid to question or think I haven't got nipples as everyone is born with them.

“Men will troll and ask about my nipples then secretly inbox me asking for topless pictures from me. It's their perverted way of trying to access topless photos of me.

“Women troll me and ask only because they are jealous and are fishing for flaws because they feel insecure about theirs.”
