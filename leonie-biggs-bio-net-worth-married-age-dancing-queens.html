Who Is Leonie Biggs? Her Net Worth, Married

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, April 3, 2024

Bravo’s Dancing Queens, which follows a group of ballroom dancers, as they compete with each other aiming for the top spots and recognition in the world of Pro-Am dance competitions, is premiering this May.

Leonie Biggs is one of the cast members of Dancing Queens. BravoTV says she “is a perfectionist who doesn’t like to take on anything unless she can be the best at it” but will she survive in the cut-throat competition of the new Bravo show? To find answers to such questions and many others surrounding her family, and net worth, read the wiki article below.

Leonie Biggs On Bravo’s Dancing Queens

Bravo’s Dancing Queens sees amateur dancers partner with professional partners and compete with each other. Colette Marotto, Donie Burch, Gaëlle Benchetrit, Leonie Biggs, Pooja Mehta, and Sabrina Strasser are 6 highly skilled amateur dancers in the new Bravo show.

The stay-at-home moms, business owners, and a finance executive put their personal and family lives aside and practice dance. “In the ballroom, they maintain their composure, but behind the scenes, the women are willing to sabotage their frenemies in the name of competition, even going as far as poaching pro partners for a chance to win,” the press release for the show said.

On April 11, 2023, Leonie shared a promotional video of Dancing Queens and captioned it, “Step into the drama of ballroom dancing with the series premiere of #DancingQueensBravo May 9th on @BravoTV! after @therealhousewivesofnewjersey.”

As per Leonie’s bio on Bravo, she has a new professional partner, Koysta Samarskyi, this dance season. Koysta adds “stress, doubt, and additional hours of practicing” in Leonie’s dance before she feels ready to compete.

Mourning the loss of Len Goodman who is apparently an iconic name in the world of ballroom dance, Leonie wrote, “Len Goodman will always… we were lucky to have him as part of our ‘Dancing with the Stars’ family for 31 seasons. He was warm and caring, and always delivered genuine guidance…,” on Instagram on April 25, 2023.

Is Leonie Biggs Married?

Yes, the Dancing Queens star Leonie Biggs is married and her husband is Nigel Biggs. The two reportedly married in January 2010.

Celebrating their 12th wedding anniversary on January 31, 2022, Leonie penned a heartfelt post on Facebook writing how they were off in different directions to see the world in the summer of 2002–Leonie’s plan was to journey to Melbourne and Nigel’s was to Canberra–and now 20 years later, they were celebrating their 12 years of marriage.

Talking about Nigel’s work, he has been working as a Director at PwC Consulting since June 2016. Prior to that, he was a Senior Manager at PwC Consulting. For education, Nigel attended Curtin University and Trinity College, Perth.

Perth native Nigel celebrates his birthday on the 1st of September every year.

Nigel and Leonie have a daughter, Charlotte, from their marriage. On October 31, 2018, Leonie shared a picture of Charlotte with the caption “Happy #halloween2018 #princesssofia #princesssofiathefirst,” on Facebook.
