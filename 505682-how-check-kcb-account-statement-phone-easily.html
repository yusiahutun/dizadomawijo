How to check your KCB account statement on your phone easily

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, September 17, 2024

KCB is one of the top and most trusted banks in Kenya. It offers a range of financial products like loans, insurance, debit and credit cards, investments, and saving accounts. The bank has a mobile app and an SSD code for non-KCB and KCB customers that offer a one-stop solution for all banking and financial needs, including statements. So how can you check your KCB account statement on your phone?

A bank statement is a monthly financial document that offers a summary of all your account activities. KCB generally prepares statements for its customers at the end of each month. You can find your statement from any KCB bank branch using a USSD code or online via the KCB app or online banking.

How to check bank statement from your phone using the KCB USSD code

Using an SSD code, you can quickly check your KCB account statement on your phone. Here are the steps.

How to check KCB statement online

It’s possible to check full bank statement online through the mobile app or KCB online banking (KCB iBank). But you must first register for the mobile app or KCB Internet banking from any KCB branch to use them.

KCB mobile app

Here is how to check your account statement using the KCB mobile app.

  • Download the mobile app from PlayStore or use USSD (*522#) for iPhone.
  • Log into the app using your KCB account number and pin. You’ll receive a one-time OTP code for verification.
  • After you’ve successfully logged into the app. Click on “Account”
  • Enter your KCB account pin (You should be able to see your bank balance)
  • Click on “Accounts”
  • Tap on “Statements”

KCB online banking

KCB online banking is a simple, secure, and hassle-free Internet banking service available to all KCB Banks customers. Here is how to check your full statement using KCB iBank.

  • Log into the KCB system using the User ID given during registration and the password sent.
  • Once logged in to the KCB system, click "Account Information." A drop menu will appear.
  • Click "Account Statement." (You'll view all the statements of your account for up to a maximum of 12 months.)

Frequently asked questions

  • How can one get their KCB full account statement online? You can get your full account statement online through KCB Internet banking. However, note that charges might apply.
  • Can you get a PDF of the KCB statement? Yes, you can get your bank statement as a PDF by visiting any KCB bank branch.
  • Can you walk into a KCB bank and ask for a statement? Yes, you can go to any KCB bank branch and ask them to print and stamp your statements to mark them as official.
