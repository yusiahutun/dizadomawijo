REPORTElle Macpherson and Billionaire Husband Jeff Soffer to Divorce After Four Years of Marriage

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Oh no!

Elle Macpherson and her husband of four years, Jeff Soffer, are headed for divorce, according to Page Six. “Elle and Jeff are definitely over,” an insider revealed to the site on the evening of Monday, June 12. The star has yet to officially confirm the divorce claims.

“She has left their house at Indian Creek in Miami with her kids, and is looking for her own house in Miami. He has been linked to some other women, but insists they are just friends,” the source added.

Elle and Jeff in May 2015.

It could shape up to be a messy split considering property tycoon Jeffrey is worth $1 billion and Elle — known in her catwalk heyday as “The Body” — reportedly has a cool $70 million in the bank.

The 53-year-old star first met Jeffrey in 2010 and they dated for two years before going their separate ways in March 2012.

Elle then dated banker Roger Jenkins, but they eventually split and the supermodel rekindled her romance with Jeffrey before they tied the knot in an intimate Fijian ceremony in July 2013.

Last year, the model opened up about their family dynamic. “I don’t believe in telling him how to raise his kids,” she explained to Stellar magazine. Jeff is a father two daughters and a son.

“We have a deep respect for each other’s profound commitment to parenting, so we would both probably say that the children come first,” Ellle continued. “And that means, at times, that we are separated because of where the kids are and it takes quite a mature person to allow each other to do what we need to do in our families’ lives.”

Elle was previously married to Elle magazine’s creative director Gilles Bensimon for three years from 1986 until 1989. In 1995, she became engaged to financier Arpad Busson, and the pair had two sons — Arpad and Aurelius
— but they split in 2005.
