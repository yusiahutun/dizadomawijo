Were Obsessed: In Praise of Paris

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, October 6, 2024

Consider it a chic melding of American sensibilities and Parisian insouciance; Madewell is launching its second collaboration with French cult brand Sézane July 15. It's New York street-wear meets Gallic romance—and if the first collection is any indication, it will not remain in stores long. From high-waisted denim to filmy blouses, hats made for picking lavender and ethereal dresses created to be paired with athletic trainers, the collection hits all the right notes. We have our eye on a striped tee that puts our affections into words: "Paris, Mon Amour."

Madewell et Sézane shirt, $58, madewell.com, available tomorrow.
