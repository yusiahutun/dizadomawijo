Who is Andrew Garfields new professional witch girlfriend, Dr Kate Tomas? The Amazing Spide

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, May 1, 2024

The news comes just two months after Garfield, 40, was photographed kissing Sports Illustrated model Olivia Brower.

So who’s his new partner – and what does she do for a living exactly?

Did Kanye West just charm his wife Bianca Censori’s mum in Los Angeles?

Dr Kate Tomas is from the UK

According to People, Tomas grew up in the UK. Her website adds that she used to work above a crystal shop in Glastonbury, where she would do tarot readings for clients from all around the world.

She ‘developed powerful intuitive abilities’ from an early age

Per her website bio, Tomas “developed powerful intuitive abilities that kept [her] safe in a very unsafe environment” from a young age. By the time she turned 19, she had started her own “spiritual apprenticeship in the Western esoteric tradition”, while working towards a degree in classics at the University of Kent.

She has an impressive educational background

Tomas has worked hard to get where she is today. As stated on her website, she holds a master’s in the philosophy of religion, also from the University of Kent, and a doctorate in philosophical theology from the University of Oxford.

“My intellectual curiosity, and dissatisfaction with the platitudes and uncritical engagement of much of the spiritual and self-help industry, led me to apply to do a PhD in philosophy, and when the University of Oxford chose me from 7,000 applicants for one of two places, I left London and moved to Oxford to study,” continues the bio.

She is a “magickal” practitioner

Tomas is a self-styled “magickal practitioner”. Her website adds that she is a “world expert in mysticism and Catholic women mystics” and started a group mentorship programme, The Spiritual Life Upgrade, in 2018, to “give women and non-binary people access to the core teachings of my spiritual tradition”. It has since been renamed the Foundations of Energy Mastery.

Per Tomas’ TikTok bio, she is a self-professed “professional witch and mentor; philosopher, artist”.

It’s unclear how Garfield came to meet Tomas, but her website notes that she is a “trusted adviser to governments, CEOs of banks, as well as people wanting advice on love and relationships”.

Who else has Andrew Garfield dated?

The Amazing Spider-Man actor has dated plenty of famous women in the past. According to Us Weekly, he has been linked to Westworld’s Shannon Woodward, Poor Things’ Emma Stone, Jessica Jones’ Susie Abromeit, singer Rita Ora, Irish actress Aisling Bea, and models Christine Gabel and Alyssa Miller.

His most high-profile relationship was with Stone, his Amazing Spider-Man co-star, with the pair dating from 2011 to 2015. It seems like they ended on a good note though, according to Garfield’s interview on Vanity Fair’s Little Gold Men podcast in January 2017. “There’s so much love between us and so much respect. … I’m her biggest fan as an artist. So for me, it’s been bliss to be able to watch her success and watch her bloom into the actress that she is. And it’s been wonderful to have that kind of support for each other. It’s nothing but a beautiful thing,” he shared.

These days, the English actor seems to prefer a low profile when it comes to his love life. “I’m not in the public eye to a great degree because I’ve designed it that way for myself,” he explained to Bustle in November 2021. “For my work, I’m fine with it, but otherwise I fight for my right for a private, personal life. My right to be ordinary. My right to be a mess. My right to be sorrowful. My right to lose, to get it wrong, to be stupid, to be a person.”
