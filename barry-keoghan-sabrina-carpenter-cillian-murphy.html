Barry Keoghan Responds To Sabrina Carpenters Cillian Murphy Comment

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, August 16, 2024

She’s not talking nonsense here. Barry Keoghan replied to Sabrina Carpenter’s comment about fellow Irish actor Cillian Murphy. It’s obvious that the two have been poking fun at each other’s crushes in public.

The “Nonsense” artist revealed what she would do with her celebrity crush at the Vanity Fair Oscars party. “If I see Cillian Murphy, I’m probably gonna leave the party. Probably just gonna leave with him,” she gushed and joked about the Oppenheimer actor who won Best Actor at the awards show that night. “I’m not gonna leave with him. I’m just gonna leave at the same time as him so I can see where he lives. So I can, like, follow his car.”

Barry Keoghan commented on Vanity Fair’s Instagram, “🙋🏻☘️.” Among those in the comment thread, another fan wrote, “She’s collecting irishmen as if they were infinity stones 😂😛”

The party couple went viral on X (formerly Twitter) after Keoghan was filmed looking at his reported girlfriend. “the way barry keoghan can’t look away from sabrina carpenter makes my standards even higher,” one user posted. One user captured what the Saltburn star said on the carpet about Carpenter, “‘oh my god’ and ‘sorry im distracted’ while looking at sabrina… oh barry keoghan i am loving your game.”

Barry Keoghan and Sabrina Carpenter were rumored to be linked in December 2023 after reportedly meeting at the Givenchy show during Paris Fashion Week. The two have hinted at their relationship with each other through subtle hints in her “Nonsense” outros during her performances and Keoghan wearing a friendship bracelet with Sabrina’s name on it. Keoghan was seen supporting the singer when she opened for Taylor Swift’s Eras Tour in Singapore. The Vanity Fair Party marks the first time that the two were spotted together.
