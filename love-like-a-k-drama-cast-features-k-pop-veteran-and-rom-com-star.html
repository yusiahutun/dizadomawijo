Love Like A K-Drama cast features K-Pop veteran and rom-com star Mon 27 November 2023 10:20

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 26, 2024

Netflix’s new dating show, Love Like A K-Drama, follows eight hopeful actors as they land roles in romantic K-dramas while searching for love. For the first time ever, the series will reveal whether the cast members’ on-screen chemistry will translate into reality.

We’re used to the tantalizing romance of K-Dramas, but what happens off-screen? Love Like A K-Drama promises to capture the heartbreak and romance of eight Korean and Japanese actors as they live under one roof as cameras stop rolling. Previous Netflix Korean dating shows include Singles Inferno, a flirty series in which contestants aim to escape “Hell” island, but Love Like A K-Drama will take a more wholesome approach.

Love Like A K-Drama season 1 cast

Song Ji Hyuk

View Instagram Post

Age: 36

Job: K-Pop idol and actor

Instagram: @xornjsz1

Born on July 14, 1987, singer and actor Song Ji Hyuk is the oldest male cast member. The 36-year-old debuted as a member of the six-member boy group, Supernova, in 2007. Supernova has been mainly active in Japan in recent years but previously collaborated with T-ara in 2009.

Ji Hyuk made his acting debut in the 2010 film, A Love Song To You, and recently appeared in the historical romance series, The Secret Romantic Guesthouse (2023), starring The Glory’s Shin Ye Eun.

Kim Won Shik

View Instagram Post

Age: 29

Job: Actor

Instagram: @shigishigi93

Sparks are guaranteed to fly on screen thanks to Kim Won Shik, the most experienced actor in the cast. The 29-year-old may have only four credits in his portfolio but they’re all K-Drama favorites. He is best known for portraying a mage in the hit fantasy series, Alchemy Of Souls, and for guest appearances in romantic comedies True Beauty and What’s Wrong With Secretary Kim?

Lee Tae Gyun

View Instagram Post

Age: 26

Job: Model and actor

Instagram: @leetg_

Described by Netflix as eccentric, Lee Tae Gyun is a model who is new to the acting world. He has modelled for the likes of GQ Korea, Dazed, and Vogue Korea.

Kim Dong Kyu

Age: 28

Job: Model and actor

Instagram: @d.onk

Born June 21, 1995, Kim Dong Kyu is a model who made his acting debut in 2018. His first role was alongside Squid Game star Lee Yoo Mi in Something Between Us, Comic Book Cafe. That same year, he appeared in Wind Blowing in Jeju, another lead role. Dong Kyu’s last acting credit was Dating Class (2019), a youth romance drama.

Rio Yamashita

View Instagram Post

Age: 31

Job: Actress and model

Instagram: @rio_yamashita_official

Twitter: @rio_y10

The oldest and most experienced actress, Rio Yamashita, hails from Tokushima, Japan. She entered the entertainment industry after auditioning for a talent agency and eventually became a model in 2007. She landed her first leading role in 2008’s Love Letter and has since appeared in at least 15 productions, including the TV adaptation of the Gannibal manga series. It premiered on Disney Plus in December 2022.

Ayano Kudo

Age: 27

Job: TV personality and actress

Instagram: @ayano_kudo_official

Twitter: @ayano_kudo05

Actress Ayana Kudo joined the media industry over 13 years ago after winning the beauty pageant, Japan Bishojo Contest, in 2009 at age 12. TV credits include the dramas Cinderella Date, Limit, and Suzuki Sensei. She also starred in the film adaptation of the children’s horror series Kaidan Restaurant.

Nozomi Bando

Age: 26

Job: Actress, model, and dancer

Instagram: @nozomi_bando_official

Twitter: @nozomi_bando94

Born September 4, 1997, the 26-year-old made her debut as an exclusive model for Hanachu, a Japanese teen magazine. If Nozomi looks familiar, you may have seen her performing as a member of the Japanese girl groups, Flower and E-Girls. Although not a singer, her dancing abilities were recognized and she remained a member until their disbandments in 2019 and 2020, respectively.

She has appeared in at least 23 TV series and films.

Honoka Kitahara

Age: 25

Job: Actress

Instagram: @honoka_kitahara

Twitter: @KitaharaHonoka

Honoka Kitahara may be the youngest member, but she has over one decade of acting experience under her belt. The 25-year-old was born June 5, 1998. Acting credits include The Night I Became An Animal, Happy Mail, and Watashi ga Kemono ni Natta Yoru 2.

Who are the hosts of Love Like A K-Drama?

Japanese actress Yko Fueki, known as Yumin in South Korea, will be hosting the show alongside studio members Haruna Kondo of the comedy duo, Harisenbon; J-pop idol A-Chan of Perfume; and comedian Shusuke Fukutoku of Jaru Jaru.

Generations From Exile Tribe dancer Hayato Komori and Japanese actress Maria Tani are also part of the lineup.
