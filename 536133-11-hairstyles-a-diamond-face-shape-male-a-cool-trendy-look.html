11 hairstyles for a diamond face shape male for a cool and trendy look

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, September 13, 2024

The diamond face shape is widest at the cheekbones and slightly narrow at the chin and forehead. When choosing a hairstyle for this face shape, you need to choose the one that will make the forehead appear equal in width to the cheekbones. But which are the best hairstyles for a diamond face shape male for a cool and trendy look?

When choosing a hairstyle, there are several things you should consider. Identifying your shape face is key to helping you find the best hairstyle that suits your head and unique features. There are so many face shapes: oval, rectangular, round, square, heart, oblong, and pear. The right hairstyle should accentuate your facial features.

List of hairstyles for a diamond face shape male

If you're hunting for a perfect haircut for your diamond-shaped face, there are so many options to choose from. According to Brio4life, these are some of the best hairstyles for a male with a diamond shape.

1. Faux Hawk hairstyle

This is the most common hairstyle for a diamond-shaped face. The hairstyle can easily be distinguished from other hairstyles because it features long spikes at the top of the head. But the hair on the sides and back should be shorter.

This hairstyle adds more volume to the top of the head, thereby emphasizing the cheekbones. The faux hawk hairstyle is mainly adorned by young people but is ideal for any man shopping for a top-notch hairstyle.

2. Quiff hairstyle

The quiff hairstyle is almost similar to the faux hairstyle as it requires the stylist to add more volume to the top of the head. But, the hair at the top should be lifted and swept back instead of being left to be in the upright position.

This hairstyle balances out the broader cheekbones as it adds height to the top of the head. The bulky top will give your forehead a broader appearance. The hair on the sides should be kept short and tapered to balance the head. The end result is a classic hairstyle that will effortlessly balance your facial features.

3. Pompadour hairstyle

Men with diamond-shaped faces have been rocking this timeless hairstyle for decades. To achieve this look, leave the hair at the top to grow long enough to be swept to the back. Then, style it into a raised and voluminous look.

The hair on the sides and at the back should be shorter and faded. This hairstyle is ideal for the bold, modern gentleman who wants to make a statement with his hair.

4. Long side-part hairstyle

The long side-part hairstyle has been popular since the 1940s. Versatile and timeless, this hairstyle can make any man look important and dignified. This hairstyle is ideal for men with long hair.

To achieve this look, comb the hair to the side and leave the remaining hair to flow naturally. You can finish the look by adding a fade to the back and sides. The trendy side part makes the style look even more dapper.

5. Undercut hairstyle

If you are looking for a trendy hairstyle for men with a diamond face, this is the ideal option for you. The key feature of this hairstyle is shorter sides and volume on the top of the head.

If you want to balance your face, the length of the hair on top of the head should either be medium or long. You can finish off the look by adding a side part to it, or you can sweep the hair to one side. This will play a critical role in balancing the width of your cheeks.

6. Man bun hairstyle

The long hairstyles for diamond shape male, like the man bun, are perfect for this face shape since the goal is to make the forehead looks wider like the cheekbones. A man bun is an excellent choice for this face shape since it softens the facial angles.

You should leave the hair to grow long enough to be tied into a man bun. It is imperative that you position the bun on the top of the head around the crown area. The hair should be bulky enough to add volume to the sides of the face.

7. Sweeping fringe hairstyle

This is one of the trendy medium-length hairstyles for a diamond face shape male. The fringe haircut is the game changer in the men's grooming world. To achieve this look, you should create soft layers on the front part of the head while shifting attention to the forehead.

If your hair is thick and wavy, you can opt for a looser fringe with lots of texture. It is ideal for any man looking for a hairstyle that perfectly blends tradition with trendiness.

8. Classic side-part

As far as diamond-shaped face hairstyles go, you can never go wrong with the classic side part. The hairstyle is ideal for you if you want to enhance your facial features by adding texture and depth. It takes many forms and flatters all textures and lengths.

To achieve this look, make a side part in your hair and comb one side, leaving the rest of the hair to fall naturally. This sleek style is mostly paired with a tapered back and tapered sides.

9. Crew cut

This is one of the best short hairstyles for a diamond face shape male. A crew cut is a short haircut with a horizontal side profile. To achieve this look, leave the top to be longer in the front and decrease in length toward the back. You can finish off the look by adding a fade or taper to it. This hairstyle looks bold and edgy, so you can never go wrong with it.

10. Long slick back hairstyle

Are you looking for unique hairstyles for a diamond face shape male with straight hair? The long, slick back hairstyle is ideal for you. To achieve this look, comb your medium or long hair back to balance the face angles. This hairstyle is ideal for men who want a cool, versatile, and sleek look.

11. Textured crop

Textured crop hairstyles for men have been gaining popularity in recent years. This cut features short hair on the sides and back, with slightly longer hair on top that can be styled in diverse ways.

The shorter sides create definition and contrast against the textured top, allowing for versatility in styling. This style is perfect for men with thin or fine hair. The added texture creates the illusion of volume and thickness.

Final word on hairstyles for a diamond face shape male

These are the best hairstyles for a diamond face shape male. The hairstyles will make your head look more balanced, thereby enhancing your appearance. Whether you prefer a short, long, edgy, or modern hairstyle, you'll find one that complements your facial features and suits your preferences from the above list.
