Promoter 101 # 46 Mike Luba, Jason Brandt &amp; Becca Leifer, AGs Trevor Swenson, Jason Kupperman

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, September 18, 2024

This week on Promoter 101 Madison House Presents’ Mike Luba, Jason Brandt & Becca Leifer share the story of Forest Hills Stadium.

Artery Global’s Trevor Swenson is more than an agent, he is an entrepreneur on the move.

Paradigm’s Jason Kupperman shares one of his all time favorite stories, plus Steiny is joined by special guest co-host Jon Topper they will break down the News of the Week together.

The Picture From Bottom Left Corner moving Clockwise: Artery Global’s Trevor Swenson, Artist Manager Jon Topper, Paradigm’s Jason Kupperman, and Madison House Presents’ Mike Luba.
