Austin Butler can't keep his hands off girlfriend Kaia Gerber at Time 100 Gala

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, May 9, 2024

Austin Butler cozied up to his girlfriend Kaia Gerber at the Time 100 Gala last night, where they couldn’t keep their hands off each other.

The Dune star joined a celebrity-studded guest list for the glitzy event at the Jazz at Lincoln Center. Hosted by Taraji P Henson, it celebrates Time’s list of 100 of the world’s most influential people. Movie stars, icons, leaders and those making changes across the globe are invited, with Dua Lipa one of the honorees performing for the night. Although it’ll air on ABC on May 12, and Hulu the following day, we have a sneak peek at some of the stars to grace the Red Carpet. And by how Austin Butler held onto his girlfriend at the Time 100 Gala, things seem to be going well.

Austin Butler attends Time 100 Gala with his model bae

The Elvis actor, who has been dating Gerber since 2021, have become one of Tinsel Town’s hottest celebrity couples.

The Hollywood star was linked to the model since late 2021, but they didn’t go public until the following year.

The 32-year-old doesn’t seem to be shy when it comes to showing her off, either.

Austin Butler and Kaia’s appearance at the Time 100 Gala, included the two staring into each other’s eyes on the Red Carpet in front of flashing bulbs. Butler held his hand around Kaia’s waist as they looked lovingly at one another.

It’s not the first time they enjoyed a steamy PDA session. They’re often snapped locking lips in public, and even at events for the stars.

Austin Butler and Kaia shared a kiss at his Elvis premiere, as well as Cannes Film Festival – so it’s not just the Time 100 Gala.

It seems they don’t mind who’s watching!

Two years and counting

The Masters of the Air star was first seen attending a yoga class together in LA, days before Christmas 2021. The Zoey 101 actor and model were then seen packing suitcases into a car before jet-setting off to the airport for a romantic trip.

Kaia, who previously dated Pete Davidson, is the daughter of supermodel Cindy Crawford who was an icon in the 1980s, and business mogul Rande Gerber.

Although they were seen spending their first Valentine’s Day in London, it wouldn’t be until the following month when they went ‘official’. They attended the pre-Oscars Best Performances bash together, walking hand-in-hand.

Then in May, they teamed up for the Met Gala, although walked the Red Carpet separately as Butler attended the glam night with Elvis Presley‘s widow, Priscilla.

Aside from the showbiz events, they also pair up for good causes, volunteering at a Children’s hospital in LA in March last year.

It seems to be going well for the duo. We can’t see where they’ll be next!
