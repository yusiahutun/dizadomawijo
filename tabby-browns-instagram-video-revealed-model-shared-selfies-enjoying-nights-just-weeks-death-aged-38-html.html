Tabby Brown's last Instagram video revealed: Model shared selfies enjoying nights out just weeks bef

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, October 8, 2024

Playboy model Tabby Brown shared selfies on Instagram enjoying nights out just weeks before her death.

Tabby, who dated Premier League stars Raheem Sterling and Mario Balotelli, died aged 38 with the news being confirmed by her former manager today.  

The Bachelor star, from south London, appeared in advertisements for Canon, Virgin Atlantic, AXE and Lynx as well as Channel 5's reality TV show. She also featured in music videos for Snoop Dogg, Dizzee Rascal and B.O.B.

Tabby was pictured smiling with friends on nights out while others show her posing in lingerie and skin-tight outfits. 

In one of her last Instagram story posts the model is wearing light-blue denim jeans and a white top as she holds up her phone while standing next to a friend dressed in a near identical outfit. 

While the last video just six weeks ago shows her appearing to be happy as she stands in front of a mirror in a public bathroom.
