Nicole Fegan's bio

Nicole Paige Fegan was born on 11 June 1989 in Atlanta, Georgia, United States, meaning she is 34 years old as of 2024. Not much is known about her parents, siblings, or early life. However, her parents were both lawyers.

The American lawyer obtained a bachelor's degree in political science and history in 2011 from Drexel University. Later, she joined John Marshal Law School in Atlanta, Georgia, earning her Juris Doctor degree in 2014.

Nicole Fegan's professional career

Is Nicole Fegan a real attorney? Yes, Nicole Fegan is a real attorney based in Atlanta. She specialises in criminal defence trials. The famous lawyer began her law career at Shah Law Firm, where she served as an attorney and legal intern between 2012 and 2014.

She also worked as a pro bono lawyer at Innocent Project, a non-profit organisation that works to exonerate wrongfully convicted people. Nicole established her own criminal defence law firm, Fegan Law LLC, in 2014, representing defendants in criminal cases.
