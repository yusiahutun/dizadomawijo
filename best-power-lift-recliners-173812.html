19 Best Power Lift Recliners That Help You Stand Up with Ease

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, May 27, 2024

If you or a loved one find yourselves with limited mobility, purchasing a power lift recliner can be a game changer. Contrary to popular belief, these chairs are not only helpful for seniors or elderly relatives. They’re for anyone who experiences difficulty sitting down and getting back up again, whether it’s due to an accident, operation, illness, or otherwise. Apart from being a super comfortable addition to any home, the best power lift recliners are adaptable, versatile, and extremely user-friendly.

What are power lift reclining chairs?

A power lift reclining chair is an armchair that lifts and slants to help the user get in and out of a seated position. They also recline backwards into a lying position for relaxing or sleeping. Needless to say, these nifty chairs have become increasingly popular for elderly customers or those with limited mobility. As our loved ones get older, simple things like sitting and standing up again can become quite difficult, but with one of the best power lift recliners on hand, you can rest easy knowing that they’ll be able to help themselves and remain independent.

Does Medicare cover lift chair recliners?

Believe it or not, you could get a portion of the cost of a power lift reclining chair covered by Medicare. A reclining lift chair is made up of two parts: the actual chair and the lifting mechanism. If you have a note from your doctor that says the lift chair is medically necessary and will help your condition, Medicare may help cover the costs of the lifting mechanism as durable medical equipment (DME). Be sure to discuss your options with your Medicare representative and your doctor to ensure eligibility.
