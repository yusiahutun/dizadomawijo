Inside fitness model Steph Claire Smith's wedding to her childhood sweetheart

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, July 7, 2024

Fitness model Steph Claire Smith tied the knot in an outdoor wedding with her husband Josh Miller on Saturday exactly a year after the childhood sweethearts got engaged at the same park.

Steph, 25, said 'I do' wearing an Australian-made One Day Bridal two piece before exchanging it for a sultry silk cut out dress at their glamorous reception in Bearii, north of Melbourne, Victoria.

Her rock hard abs were on display in both outfits but prior to the wedding Steph insisted she wouldn't be spending more time in the gym to change her body. 

She complimented the look - and kept things comfortable - in a pair of on trend white sneakers. 

Steph's beau and business partner Josh, 29, opted for a classic black suit and bow tie that matched the six groomsman standing alongside him at their makeshift altar, set against a stunning lake.

How did Steph exercise before the wedding?

Steph said she wasn't going to up her exercise or change her diet before the wedding. 

'It kind of got me thinking about what is it that makes us feel like we have to look different,' she said.

'I don't feel like I need to change, I feel good in my own skin. I'm not changing anything at all'

'I just and enjoy the journey. I've still been eating chocolate and cheese and everything.'

In the bride's first post to her 1.5 million social media followers after the special event she called herself 'Mrs Miller' and described feeling 'so so happy' after a day of festivities.

Meanwhile Josh praised his glamorous wife in a photo of the couple kissing at the end of the aisle, surrounded by close family and friends.

'Yesterday was the most incredible day of my whole entire life! Seeing the most beautiful girl in the world walk towards me with her amazing smile was beyond special! I loved sharing my vows with you and becoming husband and wife,' the founder of Soda Shades said.

Steph chose Josh's sister Bailey as one of her bridesmaids, dressing her in a Shona Joy pastel pink gown, and her Keep It Cleaner co-founder Laura Henshaw, who got engaged three weeks after Steph to model Dalton Graham.

Event stylists Creating Beautiful were charged with the floral and reception design on their wedding day, building tepees for guests to relax in on the vast property after the formal arrangements were done.

The Voice singer Jax Thomas sang the couple's first dance song Can't Help Falling In Love by Elvis Presley before everyone danced under a string of fairy lights.

A bevy of fellow models and Instagram 'influencers' attended the wedding from all over Australia, including Jade Tunchy and her boyfriend Lachie Brycki, Brooke Hogan and Myles Pitt and Rozalia and Nick Russian.

Steph, who got engaged on Friday November 23, 2018, admitted to having a crush on Josh 'since she was 12 years old' but the couple didn't start dating officially until their late teens.

They live in Melbourne together but frequently fly to both Sydney and Melbourne for Steph's ambassador roles with Adidas and Bondi Sands fake tan.

The pair - alongside Josh's brother - co-own swimwear company Midnight Co, which combines eco-friendly fabrics with bright colours and stylish cuts.

Steph opened up about planning their wedding on the Keep It Cleaner podcast she shares with Laura Henshaw the day after she signed her marriage certificate. 

She started the day with a meditation and short yoga flow from their fitness program, pancakes and berries for breakfast and a vegetable platter for lunch.

Steph said the 'worst mistake' they made was not making a budget before planning their luxury 'glamping slash festival' wedding weekend. 

'It's just ridiculous how much more expensive something gets when the word wedding is involved,' she said. 

The newlyweds will be jetting off the Byron Bay for a 'mini moon' before celebrating their proper honeymoon in Europe next year.
