7 Best Loafer Styles to Shop Now

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 2, 2024

Best Penny Loafers

Vagabond Shoemakers Alex Loafers

Best Penny Loafers

Vagabond Shoemakers Alex Loafers

Credit: Free People

The story behind the penny loafer's name may be outdated, but the shoe itself remains a wardrobe hero. Slip this menswear-inspired pair—neutral, tailored, and slightly lifted—into your virtual cart first.

Best Penny Loafers

G.H. Bass Whitney Weejuns

Best Penny Loafers

G.H. Bass Whitney Weejuns

Credit: G.H. Bass

Bass has perfected its penny loafers through more than a century and a half of business. When they're out of stock, they're worth the wait for the truly superior fit and timeless vibe.

Best Chunky Loafers

Reformation Agathea Chunky Loafer

Best Chunky Loafers

Reformation Agathea Chunky Loafer

Credit: Reformation

Fashion has lately been fascinated with a loafer mico-trend: the platform or lug-sole loafer. These pairs take your outfits to new heights with exaggerated rubber soles and uppers in every shade and print. Reformation's croc-embossed pair are in stock for now, but their footwear is known for selling out quickly.

Best Chunky Loafers

Alohas Trailblazer Lug Sole Loafers

Best Chunky Loafers

Alohas Trailblazer Lug Sole Loafers

Stand tall in Alohas' loafers, featuring a 2" rise. Moss green functions as a neutral, but the label also carries cream, brown, and black for the color-shy.

Best Chunky Loafers

Lattelier Leather Loafers

Best Chain Loafers

Gucci Jordaan Leather Loafer

Best Chain Loafers

Gucci Jordaan Leather Loafer

Credit: Gucci

Many of classic loafers are adorned with a gold horsebit or chain on the upper—Gucci's horsebit being the most recognizable of the lot.

Best Chain Loafers

Labucq Koko Moc Ivory

Best Chain Loafers

Labucq Koko Moc Ivory

Credit: Verishop

Here's a loafer whose chain accent is only part of the appeal. The remainder comes from its slide-on, slide off fit and pumped-up soles.

Best Chain Loafers

Celine Margaret Loafer

Best Chain Loafers

Celine Margaret Loafer

Credit: Celine

If you're in the know, you know Celine's loafers are the best for a touch of unexpected gold hardware. Inside of a classic horsebit, the brand deploys a C-embossed chain to equally upscale effect.

Best Driving Loafers

Tod's Gommino Driving Shoes

Best Driving Loafers

Tod's Gommino Driving Shoes

Credit: Tod's

Driving loafers are equipped with grommeted rubber outsoles for grip and durability. Tod's is the first name to check when searching for a pair of drivers that will last you seasons (and that come in the largest spectrum of colors and patterns for personalization).

Best Driving Loafers

M. Gemi The Felize

Best Driving Loafers

M. Gemi The Felize

Credit: M.Gemi

The Felize driving loafer is M.Gemi's first and top-selling shoe. Reviewers have bought two, three, and, in one standout case, eleven pairs after falling for the fit.

Best Driving Loafers

Rothy's The Driver

Best Driving Loafers

Rothy's The Driver

If you're styling drivers for a long-haul trip, this responsibly-sourced pair is for you. Rothy's easygoing drivers are double cushioned for ultimate comfort along the soles of your feet as well as your ankles. In other words, the break-in period is a phenomenon of the past.

Best Embellished Loafers

Dear Frances Luca Loafer

Credit: Dear Frances

Best Embellished Loafers

Prada Logo-Detailed Crystal Loafers

Best Embellished Loafers

Credit: Nomasei

Kristen Stewart and Nicole Kidman are among the many celebs wearing Nomasei's eco-conscious loafers on the FYC circuit. This pair puts a twist on mixed metals with silver metallic leather and a gold embellished upper.

Best Slip-on Loafers

Margaux The Mule

Best Slip-on Loafers

Margaux The Mule

Credit: Margaux

With its padded soles and range of rich velvet and suede uppers to choose from, Margaux makes the silhouette even more suited for all-day wear.
