Jennifer Lawrence Makes Mom Jeans Look Chic on Outing with Baby Son

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, January 1, 0001

Jennifer Lawrence's Hollywood mom-off-duty look is giving us major spring street style inspiration.

The Hunger Games star was spotted out in New York City with her family yesterday and looked effortlessly cool while enjoying the sunny weather. Lawrence wore a black turtleneck sweater tucked into a pair of blue extra-wide-leg mom jeans cinched with a black leather belt. She layered on a navy blue coat and stayed comfortable in some bright green Adidas Samba sneakers.

Getty Images

The actress further accessorized with a camel leather tote bag, brown tortoiseshell sunglasses, and green-stone drop earrings. She was seen carrying a New York Yankees hat and tiny yellow jacket with a cartoon lion's face on the hood, as her husband, Cooke Maroney, walked their baby's stroller.

Getty Images

Maroney also looked casually stylish for the day out. He wore a light gray T-shirt, beige khakis, a fuzzy blue jacket, and beige-and-white Vans sneakers. He, too, accessorized with brown tortoiseshell sunglasses.

Lawrence and Maroney welcomed their first child, son Cy, in February 2022. In an interview a few months later, she spoke candidly of how much her life has changed for the better since she became a mother.

"The morning after I gave birth, I felt like my whole life had started over. Like, Now is day one of my life. I just stared. I was just so in love," Lawrence said. "I also fell in love with all babies everywhere. Newborns are just so amazing. They're these pink, swollen, fragile little survivors. Now I love all babies. Now I hear a baby crying in a restaurant and I'm like, Awwww, preciousssss."
