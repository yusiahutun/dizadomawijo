    FAQ's

    Does tattoo concealer really work?

    If you're looking for a quick fix, tattoo concealer is a wonderful option. You can use a range of concealer hues to cover your tattoos and make your skin appear more radiant.

    Is it safe to put concealer on a new tattoo?

    Although only a powerful, high-quality sunscreen can assist to keep a new tattoo safe from the sun, it is not suggested to apply any other product on the tattoo until it has fully healed. Please wait before using concealer until your tattoo is healed and you are free of rashes.

    How long does a tattoo concealer last?

    Most tattoo concealer last atleast 24 hours, but there are a few tattoo concealers that can be used for 3-4 days without fading.

    How do you use concealer to cover tattoos?

    Pick the shade that matches your skin tone or blend the light and dark shades to suit the skin tone and apply on the tattoo. Set it with setting spray or powder and voila, you are done!
