After years out of the spotlight, new dad Billy Zane has been proudly showing off his family as of late.

The 46-year-old, who famously played Titanic villain Caledon Hockley in 1997, proudly smiled as he strolled along at the Grove in Los Angeles on Sunday with his glamorous girlfriend Candice Neill and their one-year-old daughter Eva Katerina.

Little Eva couldn't keep her eyes off her dad, who looked suave in a grey jacket layered over a blue sweater and open-buttoned shirt with jeans and sneakers.

The 25-year-old model coordinated with the actor, wearing a grey crop jacket over jeans and knee-high leather boots.

Eva kept warm in layers as she was carried along by her mum, dressed in a grey hooded sweatshirt, black pants and pink sneakers to match her pink hair bow.

The Titanic star and his partner of three years have been stepping out often after years of keeping away from the limelight.

The fashionable family man is definitely primed to return to the screen, as his IMDB profile says he completed a whopping 11 films this year and has another five slated for release in 2013.

Zane recently starred in Elektric Children, which opened in Britain in July and also stars Rory Culkin.

In the film, the actor star plays a strict Mormon father living in a fundamentalist Utah community.

While it's been a busy acting year for Zane, who's recently finished The Mule with Sharon Stone and Two Jacks with Sienna Miller, the actor has also been working on his artistic side.

Zane has shared on his Twitter account many of his abstract paintings -- including one that sold earlier this year for $4500.
