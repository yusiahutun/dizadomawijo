6 of Zo Kravitz and Channing Tatums best couple fashion looks: from their Rosemarys Baby Hal

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 26, 2024

Hollywood’s stylish “It” couple Zoë Kravitz and Channing Tatum are officially engaged! According to People, the news was confirmed earlier this week amid Halloween celebrations.

The Batman actress and the Magic Mike’s Last Dance actor, who have been romantically linked since the summer of 2021, were photographed leaving a party, with Kravitz seemingly donning an engagement ring on her finger. Happy days!

Those who have been keeping up with Kravitz and Tatum’s relationship will have noticed that they’ve been stepping up their couples fashion game lately too.

Here are their best fashion moments together so far, ahead of their to-be-confirmed wedding.

1. Rosemary’s Baby for Halloween

OK, so this one doesn’t win marks for style per se, but we loved the humour-meets-horror element of it. Tatum in a onesie and baby bonnet resembling a satanic infant en route to a Kendall Jenner party? Sure! Taking inspiration from the 1986 film Rosemary’s Baby, the couple dressed the part with Kravitz donning a blonde pixie cut for the role of Rosemary Woodhouse, played by Mia Farrow in the film.

2. ‘Tis trench season!

Kravitz is the queen of cool fashion, and as autumn arrives, she’s been embracing the lower temperatures with her sartorial choices. Earlier this month, Lenny Kravitz’s daughter donned a chic beige trench coat from quintessential quiet luxury label The Row in the City of Lights during Paris Fashion Week – and her date, Tatum, followed suit in a similar style but in a darker hue.

3. Jack Antonoff’s wedding

The lovebirds went formal for the big event back in August, with Kravitz in a long black strapless dress and shades (while clutching Swift’s arm) and Tatum, also in sunglasses, going for an all-black outfit under a light green blazer.

4. Autumn vibes

Tatum and Kravitz channelled sweater weather vibes in October 2021, when they stepped out in cosy layers while out and about in New York City. The Pussy Island director wore a beanie, shades, jeans and a polo neck with a long black coat, while Tatum kept it even simpler with a hoodie and trousers.

5. Met Gala 2021

Kravitz is renowned for being bold when it comes to fashion, and her nearly naked Yves Saint Laurent dress at the Met Gala in 2021 was no exception. She wore a bejewelled mesh slip which left little to the imagination on the red carpet, and reunited behind the scenes with her handsome man, who was dressed in a smart tux.

6. That iconic bike pic

Two years ago, the lovebirds were photographed on another NYC outing that ended up going viral.

While matching in black tops and blue bottoms, Kravitz hopped on the back of her beau’s bike as he pedalled them through the city in a scene that looked like something straight out of a teen romcom.
