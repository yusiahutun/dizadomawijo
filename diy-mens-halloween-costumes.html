30 Best Men's Halloween Costumes to Buy or DIY in 2023

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

Each year, there's no shortage of fun and simple Halloween costume ideas for kids and Halloween costume ideas for women, but Halloween costume ideas for guys can be a different story. But, rest assured, there are lots of fun ideas to be had, you just have to search for them, and we've done the hard work for you. We've rounded up 24 easy, unique, and customizable Halloween costumes for men. There's a good chance you have some of these elements in your closet already, so you won't have to spend a lot of money for a cool look. Oftentimes, a pop culture Halloween costume is the way to go if you're looking to make a splash at the Halloween contest.

You can always go the Halloween superstore route or order something from Amazon and hope it arrives on time, but we find the best way to put together a fun Halloween costume for a man is to use a little creativity. And if you're going with a date, a clever couples Halloween costume is always a great away to go.

So whether it’s you, your significant other, or your son who’s on the hunt for a funny Halloween costume, we have a feeling there'll be at least one outfit on our list that spark their creativity.
