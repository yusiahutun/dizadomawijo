Beyonce wears bondage chic to party after Kanye West's NY fashion show

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, March 11, 2024

It may have been friend Kanye West's day, but as soon as the sun went down Beyonce proved who really runs this town.

The 33-year-old star strutted her stuff for hundreds of adoring fans as she headed to the 40/40 Club with beau Jay Z.

The couple was all but swarmed by the massive crowd who braved the cold to just grab a glimpse of the king and queen of hip hop.

Bow down: Beyonce took her style to a new sexy level for a night out at the 40/40 Club In New York on Thursday evening

And the Crazy In Love singer did not disappoint, as she made a dramatic costume change for her night of partying.

While Queen B had dressed in a stylish but semi sweet floral number to watch pal Kanye West's collaboration with Adidas debut during the day, there was nothing sweet about her night time look.

The star stepped out in a very saucy bondage inspired outfit featuring lace up black pants with peek-a-boo sections, a silver and black and netted it with two jackets. 

Starry arrival: The singer was joined by her husband Jay Z, who wore the same outfit of black banks, sweater, Timberland Boots and a fur jacket that he had to Kanye West's Adidas presentation earlier in the day 

I don't think you're ready: The star stepped out in a very saucy bondage inspired outfit featuring lace up black pants with peek-a-boo sections, a silver and black and netted it with two jackets 

Not afraid to take her style to another level, Beyonce wore her black trench with one side cinched in with a leather waist belt and then a second studded full length suede jacket which she wore like a cape, perfectly draped across her shoulders.

Not done there, the pop star further accessorised her look with a felt wide brimmed hat from New York's JJ Hat Center and a pair of her favourite boots.

The black rather kinky six inch heel boots by Azzedine Alaia feature two metallic buckles at the ankles, and retail at around $850 but are now sold out.

Turning up the sexy factor to a 10, the 33-year-old wore her hair in voluminous waves and added lashings of vampy dark red lipstick. 

Earlier in the day, the hip hop power couple headed to Kanye's presentation for his new collaboration.

For the event, Beyonce wore a tight matching blue top and skirt featuring embroidered flowers with a colour coordinated jacket which she wore as a cape.

The famous pair sat alongside Kim Kardashian, US Vogue editor Anna Wintour, Hailey Baldwin, and Russell Simmons.

Originally Kim and Kanye's young daughter North sat with the star-studded group, but the toddler grew upset ahead of the show and was taken backstage.

Beyonce and Jay Z seemed to be enjoying the show, sporting wide grins as they stood afterwards to give their friend a round of applause.

Crazy in love: Beyonce and Jay Z - again in Timberlands - headed to Kanye West's fashion show earlier on Thursday

At the show Kanye also debuted a new song - featuring Sia and Vic Mensa - which was called Wolves and played as the models showed off his clothing.

The 37-year-old's hotly anticipated high-tops, called the Yeezy 750 Boosts, will go on sale on Saturday, February 14 and retail at $350. 

Nine thousand pairs will initially be released, and can be reserved now.
