Who is Chris Brown Married To or Single Right Now? Also, Know about his Dating History!

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, February 19, 2024

Chris Brown is a Grammy Award-winning R&B and dance music entertainer who made headlines for assaulting ex-girlfriend Rihanna. He has been linked to a girl named Ammika Harris, but later they separated. Let’s know more about his both current and past relationship love affairs and other many interesting facts.

Is Chris Brown Single? Who is he Married To?

Chris Brown is not married yet but he was rumored to be dating a woman named Ammika Harris. Some people think she bears a striking resemblance to Breezy’s ex-girlfriend Karrueche Tran. He has been linked to his rumored ex-girlfriend Ammika Harris since the year 2015, with the couple recently being snapped in Paris together. Later, they separated in Aug 2016 after dating for 1 year.

According to who dated who records, Chris is possibly single. There is no information available about his current love life. He might be looking for a good life partner to settle down with. Whoever will be his wife she must be a lucky lady. He has kept himself busy in making his career in the music industry.

Does Chris Brown have a kid?

Chris Brown and former model Nia Guzman gave birth to a daughter, Royalty Brown on 27th May 2014. Nia got pregnant in the year 2014. The friendship between Brown and Guzman, who were friends for years before the pregnancy, didn’t lead to marriage as the two called it quits before their baby’s arrival.

Chris came to know that he was the father of Nia’s baby in the year 2015 after a DNA test proved the child came from his loins. At the time, he was in a long-term relationship with American model and actress Karrueche Tran, who dumped him immediately after he publicly acknowledged that Royalty was his child.
