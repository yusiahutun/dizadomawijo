The movie was released in 1998.

“There’s Something About Mary” first hit the theaters in 1998, captivating audiences with its hilarious and memorable moments.

Cameron Diaz’s iconic hair gel scene was improvised.

One of the most memorable scenes in the movie is when Cameron Diaz’s character, Mary, mistakes hair gel for something else. Surprisingly, this scene was improvised by Diaz herself!

Ben Stiller was the perfect choice for the role of Ted.

Ben Stiller’s portrayal of Ted, the lovable but awkward protagonist, was spot-on. His comedic timing and relatable performance contributed to the film’s success.

It was the highest-grossing comedy of 1998.

“There’s Something About Mary” struck a chord with audiences, earning over $360 million worldwide and becoming the highest-grossing comedy of 1998.

The Farrelly brothers directed the film.

The movie was directed by the talented duo, Peter Farrelly and Bobby Farrelly, known for their unique and outrageous comedy style.

The film was nominated for two Golden Globe Awards.

“There’s Something About Mary” received nominations for Best Motion Picture – Musical or Comedy and Best Actress – Musical or Comedy for Cameron Diaz’s performance.

It features an all-star cast.

Aside from Cameron Diaz and Ben Stiller, the movie also starred Matt Dillon, Chris Elliott, and Lin Shaye, among others. Their chemistry on screen added to the comedic brilliance of the film.

The word “frank” is mentioned 87 times throughout the movie.

Keep your ears open while watching the movie because the word “frank” is said a staggering 87 times! It sure is a favorite word of the characters.

Some scenes were shot in Miami, Florida.

To give the movie an authentic and vibrant feel, some scenes were filmed on location in sunny Miami, Florida.

The film’s tagline is “Love is in the hair.”

The catchy tagline “Love is in the hair” perfectly captures the essence of the movie’s humorous and heartwarming storyline.

There were several title options before settling on the final one.

The movie originally had different working titles such as “The ben and mary project” and “Ted’s syndrome” before the filmmakers finally decided on “There’s Something About Mary.”

Jon Stewart turned down the role of Ted.

Jon Stewart was initially offered the role of Ted but turned it down. Luckily, Ben Stiller stepped in and brought his own unique charm to the character.

The movie marked Cameron Diaz’s breakout role.

“There’s Something About Mary” catapulted Cameron Diaz to stardom, solidifying her as a comedic talent to be reckoned with.

Keith David provided the voiceover for the film’s trailer.

If you noticed the familiar voice in the movie’s trailer, that was actor Keith David lending his deep and authoritative voice for the promotional material.
