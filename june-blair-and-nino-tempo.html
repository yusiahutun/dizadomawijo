About

American Actress June Blair was born Margaret June Blair on 20th October, 1933 in San Francisco, California, USA and passed away on 4th Dec 2022 Canoga Park, California, U.S. aged 89. She is most remembered for Playboy's Playmate of the Month January, 1957. Her zodiac sign is Libra.
