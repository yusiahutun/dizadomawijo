Best Red and Nude Lipsticks

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, February 21, 2024

RAVISHING REDS

A swipe of crimson lipstick makes you look glamorous instantly, little to no additional makeup required. First, find your signature shade. If your complexion is medium to deep, opt for a burgundy or a red with orange undertones; if you're fair, blue-based or berry hues are ideal. Then, for lasting color, try makeup artist Erin Parsons' layering technique: "Use a small brush to apply concealer around your mouth, line your lips with pencil, apply lipstick, blot with a tissue, and reapply lipstick. The concealer creates a barrier that will hold it in place all day."

NEW NUDES

There are more neutral shade options—and textures—than ever before. Glosses and sheer formulas are always flattering, while the new velvety mattes add rich intensity. (They also feel cushiony on the lips and remain moist, a technological marvel.) Look for a your-lips-but-better hue (pinks, peaches), not a fleshy one (yellows, beiges). You don't want your lip color blending into the rest of your face, explains makeup artist Troy Surratt. Create subtle dimension and a fuller pout with his easy tip: "Line only the Cupid's bow and the lower center of the lip."
