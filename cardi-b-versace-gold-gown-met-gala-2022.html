Cardi B Wears Glimmering Gold Versace at the 2022 Met Gala

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, August 12, 2024

Cardi B knows how to work a red carpet‚Äîand this year's Met Gala was no exception.

The "WAP" rapper made a major entrance on the steps of the Metropolitan Museum of Art tonight to mark fashion's biggest night. For the evening, Cardi wore a showstopping gold glittery gown and matching gloves by Versace. For her glam, the rapper opted for neutral, '90s-inspired makeup and wore her hair in a supermodel-esque voluminous bouffant.

The appearance marked Cardi's first night back at the Met since 2019, where she stunned in a burgundy ruffled number by Thom Browne.

John Shearer//Getty Images

Twitter was quick to react to Cardi's look, describing her ensemble as one of the few of the night that brought the true essence of Gilded Glamour.

The rapper has been assuring fans that she's diligently working in the studio on new music. She's also appeared on hit features with fellow stars Lizzo, SZA, Summer Walker, and more as of late.

Cardi's biggest news thus far this year has been that she welcomed her second child, a baby boy named Wave, with her husband, rapper Offset. Cardi joked while chatting with red-carpet host La La Anthony that her voluptuous figure was thanks to her son. "This isn't lipo!" she shared during her interview.
