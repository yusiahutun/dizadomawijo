Prince Harry 'wins' big disagreement with Meghan Markle over Archie and Lilibet, source says

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, March 2, 2024

Prince Harry and Meghan Markle can’t see eye to eye over one important decision when it comes to Archie and Lilibet, a source claims.

The Duke and Duchess of Sussex have a jam-packed schedule right now. The royals are not only working on two different Netflix projects, but the 42-year-old is launching her new lifestyle brand, American Riviera Orchard. Prince Harry was recently seen playing polo in a charity match, while also working with the Invictus Games earlier this year. Meghan Markle, on the other hand, is also looking to launch a new podcast after the deal with Spotify broke down, but insiders have said it’s struggling to get past the planning stages. As the duo are working on their upcoming shows, one source has said Harry and Meghan Markle have butted heads over Prince Archie and Lilibet when it comes to the small screen.

Prince Harry and Meghan Markle ‘don’t see eye to eye’ over Archie and Lilibet choice

Filming has begun on Meghan’s new lifestyle show – one of the steps that is pinning her to be the new ‘Martha Stewart’ – and Harry’s polo series is also underway.

When the 39-year-old was spotted at a polo match a couple of weeks ago, he was being followed around by the cameras.

Although they both have new TV shows on the table, the royals apparently haven’t been on the same page when it comes to their children. A source claims Prince Harry and Meghan Markle disagree on whether Archie and Lilibet will be featured on them.

It’s believed neither the Prince or Princess will appear in them, as part of the Sussexes mega-million deal with the streaming giant.

The Sun reports disagreed over whether their family lives will be private when it comes to certain aspects with Netflix. Although the couple have been filmed inside their Montecito mansion for their six episode docu-series and Invictus Games documentary, this was off the cards.

Harry, brother to Prince William, is said to want to keep Archie and Lilibet away from cameras, but former Suits actress Meghan Markle wants the world to see them.

An insider claimed: “Harry and Meghan have not always agreed on how much they should expose their children to the media but, in this case, Harry has clearly won.”

Royal fans only see glimpses of the little ones

After Meghan and Harry left the UK for America, which is now the Duke’s official residence, they haven’t seen much of their two tots.

Only small moments in short clips or photos from behind have been shared by the duo. They did choose to hand over a large amount of home videos for their Netflix docu-series though, but it didn’t show much.

It comes as reports say Meghan’s new show won’t be filmed at their $13m mansion to avoid the two kids being shown on camera. Instead, she’ll be at a neighbor’s house in the luxury neighborhood.

An insider with connections to the palace said Prince Harry and Meghan Markle haven’t brought Archie and Lilibet back to the UK to see their royal family, which is hard for King Charles.

Meghan’s dad, Thomas, has also made pleas to meet his grandchildren.
