What is Madonna and Tokischa's relationship?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, July 5, 2024

TO COMMEMORATE Pride Month, Madonna and Tokischa came together to bring their talents live for the crowd in attendance.

By the time Madonna and Tokischa concluded their performance, they shocked their fans by sharing a kiss on stage.

What is Madonna and Tokischa's relationship?

Fans became aware of Madonna and Tokischa's friendship after the two shouted each other out on social media.

Through their online exchanges, they posted pictures of each other together in the recording studio.

Due to the mutual appreciation between the two, fans of both have speculated that they have a collaboration on the way.

On June 14, 2022, Madonna shared a photo of her and Tokischa on Instagram and captioned it, "A woman’s work is never done."

The rapper tweeted the snapshot and told fans, "I have the song on repeat," with multiple fire emojis.

What happened between Madonna and Tokischa?

Days later on Thursday, June 23, Madonna took the stage at the World of Women’s NFT event in New York City to celebrate Pride Month.

She surprised the crowd by welcoming a guest performer to the stage, who turned out to be Tokischa.

Together, the duo sang a remixed version of the Queen of Pop's Hung Up.

At various points of the concert, the two appeared to dance provocatively until the show's end.

When the concert reached its conclusion, the music stars planted a kiss on one another and began making out.

Tokischa followed up the make-out session by kneeling in front of Madonna and putting her head in the latter's genital area.

While taking part in the NSFW moment on stage, the Medellin singer quipped to the audience: "It’s a tough job, but somebody has to do it."

Several pop culture fans compared the moment to Madonna's showstopping kiss with Britney Spears at the 2003 MTV VMAs.

Who is Madonna dating?

Sometime in 2018, Madonna started dating American dancer Ahlamalik Williams.

In April 2022, the two hit splitsville after months of not being publicly seen together.

A source shared details about the split to The Sun: "Madonna has thrown herself into a busy social life and has been seeing her friends and family after the split.

"She has a packed schedule, working on her upcoming biopic, new music, and looking after her family."

The insider exclusively disclosed: "Things have been on and off with Ahlamalik for a while.

"There’s a lot of love but for now, they have decided to separate.

"They are still on good terms and there are no hard feelings but they are at different places with their lives."
