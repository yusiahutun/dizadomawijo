Sofia Vergara Suffers Nip Slip, Dances With Derek Hough at Emmys Party

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, September 25, 2024

Oopsies! Sofia Vergara tore up the dance floor at HBO’s Emmys afterparty held in the Pacific Design Center in Hollywood, when she suffered a wardrobe malfunction in the process.

The Modern Family bombshell, 42, was joined by Dancing With the Stars pro Derek Hough, who scooped her up and twirled her around late Monday, Aug. 25. An observer at the bash tells Us Weekly that Vergara was requested on the floor by Hough as soon as “Footloose” started playing.

“The two were spinning around the dance floor and she was flying through the air,” the insider notes.

Soon, a crowd gathered around the two stars and the cell phones came out to capture the crazy moment as “Hit the Road Jack” started playing.

The actress, wearing a form-fitting strapless white gown by Roberto Cavalli, got so into the dance sequence that when Hough, 29, whisked her off the floor, she accidentally flashed her nipple.

“It was a full five minutes of him spinning her around the dance floor,” the observer notes to Us. “No one could quite believe what they were seeing,” the eyewitness recalls, “nor were they able to take their eyes off of it.” One of the spectators was Hough’s younger, equally-famous sibling Julianne, who stood by the stage and eagerly clapped along to her brother dancing with Vergara.

Despite that nip flash, the gorgeous Colombian actress had an uproarious night. Earlier that evening, she hit the floor with Modern Family costar Sarah Hyland and Orange Is the New Black actress Laverne Cox, and later, with show creator Steven Levitan, Nolan Gould, and others. The group danced to “Shout,” “Do you Love Me (Now That I Can Dance)”, and “La Bamba,” the observer adds, “as if no one was watching.” At one point, they hilariously got a hold of maracas, too, and it “got wild.”
