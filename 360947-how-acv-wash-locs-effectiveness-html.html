Why raw ACV wash for locks?

Apple cider vinegar is a by-product of fermented apples. Since apples have numerous beneficial nutrients like pectin, malic acid, calcium, and potassium, the fermentation result yields an even better product full of beneficial acids and enzymes.

ACV has a PH level of 3, which is perfect for natural hair. When applied, it balances your hair acidity, leading to numerous positive effects. The rinse effects of ACV are what builds luscious locs.
