Hailey Bieber Embraces Workwear Trend in Taupe Bomber and Oversize Jeans

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, February 1, 2024

It’s hard to achieve the casual cool of looking like you just fixed a car or operated some heavy machinery, but Hailey Bieber did it with her latest outfit.

The Rhode founder is the latest star to embrace the workwear aesthetic we’ve seen growing over the past few seasons—with high fashion finding ways to embrace, meld with, and even imitate longtime workwear brands like Carhartt and Dickies.

At the center of Bieber’s outfit was a taupe bomber jacket from Meryll Rogge, which featured a caramel-colored corduroy spread collar. The piece, which had elasticized hems along the bottom and sleeves, typically includes a gray detachable hoodie too, but the model removed the drawstring hood for this look.

ShotbyNYP / BACKGRID//BACKGRID

To give the ensemble even more of a greaser vibe, Bieber layered the jacket over a white cami, and she paired these pieces with loose-fit jeans from Acne Studios. The oversize pants created a lot of volume at the bottom of her legs, where they pooled over her black square-toed loafers from Jil Sander. Bieber’s polish-finish shoes were the most business-centric part of the outfit, and they included ruched detailing around the edges, and a low block heel at the back.

Acne Studios Loose Fit Jeans

Acne Studios Loose Fit Jeans

Proving that a few solid staples can go a long way, Bieber kept it simple with her jewelry, opting for only a pair of stud earrings and thin black oval sunglasses.

Over the past few months, we’ve seen Bieber slowly incorporate more workwear into her wardrobe. In October, the model sported overalls from Dickies for a date night with her husband, Justin. She’s been no stranger to adding a casual baseball cap or leather jacket to her outfits, either.
