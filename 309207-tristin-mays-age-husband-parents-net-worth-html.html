Tristin Mays: Age, husband, parents, net worth

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Tristin Mays is an American actress, model, dancer, and singer. She is famous for being one of the top child models in America signed under the Ford Modelling Agency. Her works as an actress include playing Riley Davis in the CBS reboot of the action-adventure TV show, MacGyver. The actress has also appeared in other TV shows and films. Some include Big Time Rush (2010), Thunderstruck (2012), The Vampire Diaries (2015-2016), and Switched at Birth (2017) among others.

Tristin Mays was born on 10 June 1990 (age 29 years) in New Orleans, Louisiana, United States. She is the daughter of Michael Mays and Viveca Mays. Her mother worked as an artist, and her father worked in the military. She has an older brother called Jeryn Mays, who is an actor. Tristin Mays' ethnicity is African American-French-Indian.

Early childhood

Tristin Mays grew up in Staten Island, New York City, United States. In 2003, her family moved to Moreno Valley, California where she would later become one of the most promising actresses in Hollywood. When she was six years old, Tristin had a minor role in a television film called Harambee! and played Angel. She became a series regular in a 1997 TV show called Gullah Gullah Island portraying Shaina.

Generally, Tristin Mays spent most of her childhood on the screens. Before graduating from Vista del Lago High School, she had appeared in several films, TV shows, and commercials. She was a child model who appeared in several national commercials including Sunkist, Kraft Cheese, ROSS, Hershey’s, and McDonald’s.
