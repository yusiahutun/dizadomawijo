Who is Lex Luger?

Lex Luger is an acclaimed retired professional wrestler. His real name is Lawrence Wendell Pfohl. He was born 2 June 1958 in Buffalo, New York, United States.

While in high school, he played football in Orchard Park, New York. Upon high school graduation, he was offered a football scholarship to join Pennsylvania State University. After his first year, he transferred to the University of Miami, playing for the Miami Hurricanes.

What happened to Lex Luger after exiting WWE?

Lex’s life was filled with a surprising twist of events after exiting WWE. First, he went on the Lex Express Tour to gain more fame. Even though the World Wrestling Federation had great plans for him, he needed something extra to make him the biggest face in the company’s history.

On 3 September 1995, his contract with WWE expired after teaming up with Shawn Michaels to defeat Owen Hart and Yokozuna at a live event. The following night, 4 September, Lex surprisingly went to WCW, World Championship Wrestling, on the first episode of WCW Nitro.

His choice didn’t sit well with the WWE chairman as the wrestler was still negotiating with WWE over a new contract when he appeared on WCW Nitro. He used this move to claim his place in the wrestling world.

What happened to Lex Luger? The former professional wrestler suffered a horrific neck injury during a plane ride from Atlanta to San Francisco. Lex Luger's disease made his career take a different turn. He once spoke about the life-threatening injury, stating:

The way I had turned my neck while sitting on the plane had cut off my blood flow. It caused massive swelling from my vertebra, at the base of my neck, to my D5 in my chest. It disabled me from the neck down.

The former football player is partially incapacitated. He has, however, gained mobility with the right treatment. He suffered a spinal stroke that led to permanent paralysis, hence his confinement to a wheelchair.

Lex Luger’s wife and children

Is Lex Luger married? The former professional wrestler is divorced. He was married to Peggy Fulbright, an acclaimed real estate agent. The couple tied the knot in 1979, but they divorced in 2003 after being married for 24 years.
